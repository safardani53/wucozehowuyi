Pregnant Katy Perry looks ready to pop in a purple dress as she sings new single What Makes a Woman

Publish date: 2024-02-24

KATY Perry showed off her baby bump while performing her new single, What Makes a Woman.

The 35-year-old - who's expecting her first child with fiance Orlando Bloom - looked ready to pop in a purple chiffon dress.

Katy looked every inch the mother-to-be in her dress, which had a polka dot design and featured pleated sleeves.

She finished off her look with an embellished headband and jeweled heels.

The singer showed off her vocals with an acoustic rendition of What Makes a Woman from her new album, Smile.

Katy is expecting her first child any day now, having announced her pregnancy back in March.

Although she hasn't given her exact due date, she said in an Instagram post following her announcement: "Let's just say it's gonna be a jam-packed summer."

The Firework singer recently gave her fans a glimpse of her daughter's pink-themed nursery.

Katy said: "Hey everyone, I'm going to show you my baby room" as she carried her laptop in her hands.

Panning over some "little clothes" on display, the songstress showcased the impending little one's outfits displayed on circular hooks, including a onesie with Orlando's face all over it.

Katy then showcased the room's bubblegum pink-theme, which included a light pink recliner, crib and coordinating mobile.

And it seems Orlando is counting down the minutes till their baby's arrival, telling Jimmy Fallon: "I'm so excited to have a little daddy's girl.

"I hope she's going to love me as much as I love her. That daddy girl thing and that love of your life feeling is right around there."
