I took my kids to the best indoor aqua park in the UK - its only a fiver entry for children and i

Publish date: 2024-06-11

A WOMAN has revealed that she took her kids to the best indoor aqua park in the UK and was left very impressed.

The woman visited the London Aquatics Center - the indoor facility with two 50-metre swimming pools and a 25-metre diving pool in the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park in Stratford, London.

But she didn’t just go to the Aquatics Center to go swimming, she went to check out the Aqua Splash - a mega inflatable obstacle course.  

The woman took to social media to show off an inside look at the Aquatics Center, as she gave users a close-up at the huge aqua park that was on the pool.

In the video, we saw children going down the huge slides, before she then gave her followers a look at the swimming pool, which was reserved for lane swimming. 

The London Aquatics Center is a brilliant day out for the family, as you can swim in the pools of champions, in one of London 2012’s best-known venues.

But the Aqua Splash, an inflatable obstacle course on the iconic Olympic swimming pool, is sure to be a splash-tastic family day of fun on the water.

It is designed for children aged eight and above and adults are invited to join in the fun too, and with challenging obstacles, slides and plenty of pool space, it’s sure to be the perfect day out for the whole family.

Alongside the video, the woman attached an audio that said: “No seriously, what is this place?”

The audio continued and then confirmed: “This is heaven.”

The woman explained that to use the Aqua Splash, it is £7.50 for an adult and £5 for a child.

The Aqua Splash runs on weekends and during school holiday periods and you must book your session online.

The woman’s TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @ideevatv, has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed a whopping 179,500 views.

It has 8,191 likes, 108 comments, 2,188 saves and 1,459 shares.

Social media users were stunned at the aqua park and were eager to express this in the comments. 

One person said: “That looks fun.”

Another added: “I wanna go there.”

A third tagged a friend and posted: “Let’s go.”

Whilst another mentioned a loved one and asked: “Should we go?”

Meanwhile, one TikTok user asked: “You can stay there the entire day?” to which the social media user confirmed “No just for an hour.”

Another questioned: “Do they provide life jackets?” to which the woman replied “Yes they do.”

Whilst someone else wondered: “Do they have parking?” to which the woman explained “We went by train… you can park in Westfield and walk to the Olympic park.”
