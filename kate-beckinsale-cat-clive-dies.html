Kate Beckinsale left devastated as legendary cat Clive dies leaving her heart totally broken

Publish date: 2024-08-12

KATE Beckinsale has been left devastated after revealing her legendary cat Clive has died.

The 49-year-old took to Instagram to share the heartbreaking news, confessing that her heart is ‘totally broken’.

Sharing a snap of her fury friend, Kate said: "Clive has died.

"I don’t have it in me to make a montage or even look at pictures of him yet.

"Every single part of my house feels like he should be in it . If anyone is expecting to hear from me , you may not for a bit and I’m sorry.

"My heart is absolutely and totally broken."

Many rushed to the comments section to share their love and support.

Paddy McGuinness wrote a love heart emoji.

Corrie's Sally Carman and Seth Rogen added: "I’m so sorry."

Anya Taylor-Joy said: "Kate sending you love ♥️."

The news will come as a huge shock as she last posted about Clive two months ago.

The feline regularly appears on her Instagram feed, as well as Kate's other pets.

In August last year Kate looked in incredible shape in a pink bikini as she straddled an inflatable swan in her pool next to Clive.

Her beloved pet cat had a matching swan to himself as he floated around the pool while wearing denim bottoms.

She said: "One is never too old,too grey or too furry to discover new joys."

The actress was also seen taking Clive for a walk with him strapped to her chest.

It comes after Kate was devastated when her Maltese-Chihuahua Ingrid died aged 11 in 2020.
