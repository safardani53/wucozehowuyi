Who is Mark Margolis' son, Morgan H?

Publish date: 2024-02-02

ON AUGUST 4, 2023, it was revealed actor Mark Margolis sadly died after battling a brief illness.

The late actor is survived by his son, Morgan, who followed in his father's footsteps - here's everything we know about him.

Who is Morgan H Margolis?

Morgan H Margolis is a former actor born on October 12, 1966, in New York City.

Following in his father's footsteps to become an actor, Morgan attended the New York High School of Performing Arts.

Morgan landed his first acting role in 1989 when he took on the part of Chuck in the TV series, The Equalizer.

The famed actor went on to be in an array of films and TV shows including Star Trek: Voyager, Dexter, and 3000 Miles to Graceland.

However, Morgan's last film credit was in 2010 when he played Officer Dante in Venus & Vegas.

What does Morgan H. Margolis do for a living?

In 2008, Morgan took over the role of CEO for Knitting Factory Entertainment and has remained in this position as of August 2023.

Knitting Factory Entertainment started out as a New York City venue that hosts an array of musical and entertainment acts in 1987.

Now, the company has branched out across the United States holding offices and venues in California, Washington, and Idaho.

Apart from being a CEO of a growing company, Morgan is a husband to Jacqueline Margolis.

Together they welcomed two children throughout their marriage.

When did Mark Margolis pass away?

Morgan announced that his father, Mark Margolis died on Thursday, August 3, 2023.

According to Morgan, his father suffered a short illness before passing Mount Sinai Hospital in New York City.

Mark Margolis was widely known for his role as Hector 'Tio' Salamanca on Breaking Bad.

Additionally, the actor appeared in classic films such as Scarface, Requiem for a Dream, and Black Swan.
