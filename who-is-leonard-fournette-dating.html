Leonard Fournette Biography

Leonard Fournette III is an American football running back for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at LSU, and was drafted by the Jaguars with the fourth overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft. Fournette was a consensus All-America selection following his sophomore season in 2015, after setting school single-season records with 1,953 rushing yards and 22 rushing touchdowns, and leading the country with 162.8 rushing yards per game. Various experts regarded him as the best player in college football, comparing him to all-time great Herschel Walker.

He attended St. Augustine High School, where he became the first freshman ever to earn a scholarship offer from LSU. He was considered the nation’s top senior recruit coming out of high school by publications like 247 Sports and CBS Sports.
