Coco Jones' What I Didn't Tell You tour: How can I get tickets?

Publish date: 2024-10-01

ACTRESS and musician Coco Jones announced her first headliner tour just days after taking home the BET Award for Best New Artist.

She will be hitting the road with Ebony Riley later this year and now fans want to know how they can secure their tickets.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Coco Jones going on tour?

Jones and Riley are planning to hit the road for the What I Didn’t Tell You Tour to support the Disney alum's debut EP of the same name.

The tour was announced just days after the Bel-Air star performed her What I Didn’t Tell You song ICU at the 2023 BET Awards, where she won Best New Artist.

“Thank you so much to my culture who has stood beside me and we were raised together,” Jones said during her acceptance speech, via Rated R&B.

“It’s been a really long, long journey. Thank you so much to my team. Thank you guys for taking the chance on me. I didn’t have anything going for me at that time.”

Read More on tours

Jones' tour will kick off on August 5, 2023, and she will play shows through September.

How can I get tickets to Coco Jones' tour?

Presale tickets for Jones' tour are set to go on sale June 28, 2023, at 10am local time followed by general public sales on June 30.

Tickets can be purchased directly on Ticketmaster or on Jones' website.
