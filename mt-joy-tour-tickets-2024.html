Mt. Joy 2024 Tour: Where can I buy tickets?

Publish date: 2024-02-02

INDIE rock band Mt. Joy has unveiled the initial leg of its much-awaited 2024 headline tour.

It spans across the US, Mexico, and Canada and includes a debut at the iconic Madison Square Garden in New York City.

Where can I buy tickets to Mt. Joy's 2024 tour?

Fans can purchase tickets to all tour dates during the general sale, which started on Friday, November 10, 2023.

Tickets are available on sites like Ticketmaster, Vivid Seats, StubHub, and SeatGeek.

More ticketing information can be found on the band's official website.

Where is Mt. Joy performing?

Mt. Joy's 2024 tour includes music festivals and shows as supporting acts for artists Noah Kahan and Zach Bryan.

More on concerts and tours

The tour dates are as follows:

  • Jan. 7-8, 2024 – Out of the Blue Festival, Cancun, Mexico
  • May 31, 2024 – Oakland-Alameda County Coliseum, Oakland, California (with Zach Bryan)
  • July 10, 2024 – The Budweiser Stage, Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • July 18-19, 2024 – Fenway Park, Boston, Massachusetts (with Noah Kahan)
  • July 20, 2024 – Merriweather Post Pavilion, Columbia, Maryland
  • Aug. 6, 2024 – Surly Brewing Festival Field, Minneapolis, Minnesota
  • Aug. 8-10, 2024 – Red Rocks Amphitheatre, Morrison, Colorado
  • Aug. 13, 2024 – Granary Live, Salt Lake City, Utah
  • Aug. 15, 2024 – Outlaw Field at Idaho Botanical Gardens, Boise, Indiana
  • Aug. 17, 2024 – Doug Mitchell Thunderbird Sports Centre, Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada
  • Aug. 21, 2024 – Remlinger Farms, Carnation, Washington
  • Aug. 23, 2024 – Hayden Homes Amphitheater, Bend, Oregon
  • Sept. 13, 2024 – Moody Center, Austin, Texas
  • Sept. 15, 2024 – Ameris Bank Amphitheatre, Alpharetta, Georgia
  • Sept. 17, 2024 – Firefly Distillery, Charleston, South Carolina
  • Sept. 24, 2024 – Ascend Amphitheater, Nashville, Tennesee
  • Sept. 28, 2024 – Madison Square Garden, New York, New York

Per the band, more dates will be announced soon.

Who are the members of Mt. Joy?

Mt. Joy is composed of Matt Quinn, Sam Cooper, Sotiris Eliopoulos, Jackie Miclau, and Michael Byrnes.

Most read in Entertainment

The five-piece band was formed in 2016 by Quinn and Cooper, who met in high school in Philadelphia.

The pair found bassist Byrnes on Craigslist, and drummer Eliopoulus and keyboardist Miclau soon joined.

They signed with their label Dualtone Records in 2017 and released their self-titled debut album, Mt. Joy, in 2018.

The band has since performed in various festivals, including Lollapalooza, Bonnaroo, and ACL Fest.

They have also performed their own headlining tours across North America and Europe.

Speaking of their 2024, vocalist Quinn said in a press release: "It’s incredible to cross these milestones off the bucket list. I know we will always remember the hard work that got us here - the many rehearsals, shows, sh***y loadouts in the snow - always knowing that if we kept going, we could be one of the best bands in the world. "

"We’re a pretty humble group but we’re excited to be put on these stages where we can go prove, to anyone who doesn’t already know, that we’re one of the best out right now.”
