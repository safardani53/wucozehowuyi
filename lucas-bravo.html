Early Life of Lucas Bravo

Lucas Bravo was born on March 26, 1988, in Nice, France. His family also has a French origin.

Daniel Bravo, Lucas Bravo’s father, was a former soccer player for France. Singer Eva Bravo is his mother. Marie-Eva is the name of his sister.

Due to his father Daniel Bravo’s soccer career, which required him to travel all over Europe, Lucas moved around a lot as a child.

When his mother, Eva Bravo, enrolled him in an improv class when he was eleven years old, Lucas developed a passion for acting.

He attended the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in Los Angeles to pursue a course in theatre, film, and television. He also pursued his education at Paris’ Tiffany Stern’s Actors Factory.
