Mika Brzezinski Biography

Publish date: 2024-06-12

On the 23rd of October 1993, Mika got married to James Hoffer. James is a famous writer, director, and current TV news reporter of WABC-TV. As a married couple, they have already been parents of two sweet little baby girls.

Mika joined The Madeira School as a high school student. After her high school graduation, she joined Georgetown University. However, after being there for about two years, she got transferred to Williams College and finally graduated from there in 1989 with a degree in English. A year later in 1990, she joined ABC’s World News This Morning as an assistant. In 1991, she began working at WTIC-TV/WTIC-DT. Within a few months, she got the post of general assignments reporter. In 2000, she joined MSNBC as a co-anchor of the weekday afternoon show, HomePage. Her net worth is said to be around $20 million dollars.
