Isaiah Rashad Trending after Alleged Gay Video Leaks Online

Publish date: 2024-04-01

Rapper Isaiah Rashad is presently trending on the internet after his alleged gay video leaked out in public.

The leaked video exposed a man — who looks like the rapper — performing sexual acts with two other men.

Rashad or his team hasn’t publicly commented on the leak, but Twitterati seem convinced that it is the rapper in the video.

Who Is Isaiah Rashad?

Rashad, born Isaiah Joel McClain, is a 30-year-old rapper from Chattanooga, Tennessee.

He was raised by his mother, a hairdresser. He found his love for music after his father let him stay up late to listen to rappers like Too $hort and Scarface.

Some of his best songs are ‘Smile,’ ‘Stuck in the Mud’ featuring SZA, ‘Shot You Down’ featuring Jay Rock and Schoolboy Q, and ‘Heavenly Father.’

The rapper also has two children — a son and a daughter — but not much is known about them, as well as his partner.
