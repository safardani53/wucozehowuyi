Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe movie: trailer and release date

Publish date: 2024-05-17

ON June 2, 2022, Paramount announced the new movie Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe.

Paramount+ took to Twitter to reveal the trailer for the upcoming movie Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe.

When does Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe premiere?

The popular show Beavis and Butt-Head first aired on MTV in 1993, its final episode airing in 2011.

In 2022, the cartoon is coming back with a new movie titled Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe.

Paramount+ tweeted the news on June 2, 2022.

"Beavis and Butt-Head are back with an all-new movie. Beavis and Butt-Head Do the Universe premieres June 23, exclusively on #ParamountPlus #beavisandbutthead"

Fans of the show can expect the movie to arrive on June 23, 2022.

The trailer begins with suspenseful music and states that "On June 23 the earth will get smarter."

Butt-Head then chimes in saying "the earth sucks."

"Beavis and Butt-Head are back with an all-new movie penetrating space," the trailer adds.

The end of the trailer tells the viewer to "witness the dumbest science fiction movie ever made."

A few other movies based on the show are Beavis and Butt-Head Do America (1996) and Beavis and Butt-Head Do Christmas (1995).

Beavis and Butt-Head was created by director and voice actor Mike Judge.

Some spin-off series that followed Beavis and Butt-Head include King of the Hill, Daria, and The Goode Family.

Who is in the cast?

Judge, 59, voiced, produced, and directed the upcoming film.

He famously voices both characters of Beavis and Butt-head.

In addition, the movie is executive-produced by Judge Lew Morton and Mike Rotenberg, and Chris Prynoski, Shannon Prynoski, Ben Kalina, and Antonio Canobbio for Titmouse.

The full cast on the movie's official IMDb only states Judge as a cast member.

How can I watch Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe?

Viewers can watch Beavis and Butt-Head Do The Universe exclusively on Paramount+.

It will only be available to stream on the platform's streaming site.
