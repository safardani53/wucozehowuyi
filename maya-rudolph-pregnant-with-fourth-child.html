Maya Rudolph: Pregnant with Fourth Child!

Publish date: 2024-09-14

It sounds like Maya Rudolph will soon be up all night once again, for the most miraculous reason possible:

The actress is pregnant!

Multiple sources confirm that Rudolph is expecting her fourth child with writer/director Paul Thomas Anderson, as the couple is already parents to Pearl, 7, Lucille, 3, and Jack, 19 months.

 

The exciting news comes on the heels of a questionable time in the star’s professional life.

Christina Applegate has dropped out of NBC’s Up All Night after the network chose to change the creative direction of the sitcom, making it into a multi-camera series in front of a live studio audience.

The future of the program is now unclear, with Rudolph’s pregnancy only complicating issues.
