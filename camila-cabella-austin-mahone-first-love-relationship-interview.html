Camila Cabello Says Austin Mahone Was Her ‘First Love’ & Kiss In New Interview

“I don’t regret anything. It was my first kiss, my first love,” Camila admitted to Seventeen about Austin. “It’s important to learn about liking somebody and being swept away, but it wasn’t meant to be.”

Wow, at just 18 years old, Camila really knows how to handle a breakup! Getting over a guy can be really hard — especially when they move on to someone else (ahem, Becky!), but the 5H frontwoman is taking it like a pro. In fact, she’s already thinking about her next boyfriend.

“I’ll find somebody else,” the Latina beauty said, proving she’s a hopeless romantic. “It’s a cycle. I love love. I’m, like, obsessed with it.”

We’re so happy Miss Cabello is keeping her head held high — even after Austin disrespected her! It’s no surprise Camila is able to maintain such a sunny disposition. She’s got the most amazing support group around her: the Fifth Harmony girls and BFF, Taylor Swift, 25. Talk about #SquadGoals, right?!
