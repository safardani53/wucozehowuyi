Thundercat tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Publish date: 2024-06-27

MANY artists are embarking on tours this year, including the bassist known as Thundercat.

The artist announced his tour on Twitter on May 4, 2022.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

How can I buy tickets?

The tickets for Thundercat's tour are already on sale on multiple platforms.

Ticketmaster is the primary website where fans can purchase tickets reliably.

Fans can also visit the artist's website directly for the tour tickets at theamazingthundercat.com/tour.

He announced the tour with an announcement on Twitter with the full tour schedule and a promotional image.

"IM GOING ON TOUR H*E," Thundercat wrote.

The tour will kick off in North America starting on July 23, 2022.

Some of the tour shows will be in support of the Red Hot Chili Peppers.

In July, the first show will be along with the Red Hot Chili Peppers in Denver, Colorado.

What are the stops on tour?

Thundercat's tour will commence in Colorado and end in Arkansas.

The following are just some of the cities where Thundercat will be performing in 2022.

  • Denver, CO - Empower Field at Mile High
  • Las Vegas, NV - Brooklyn Bowl
  • San Diego, CA - Petco Park
  • Santa Clara, CA - Levi’s Stadium !
  • Helena, CA - Blue Note Jazz Festival Napa
  • Los Angeles, CA - SoFi Stadium
  • Seattle, WA - T-Mobile Park
  • Vancouver, British Columbia - The Orpheum
  • Salt Lake City, UT - Union Event Center (The Union)
  • Kansas City, MO - Grinders KC 

The full tour schedule and dates can be found on Thundercat's website or Ticketmaster.

When was Thundercat's latest release?

Two years ago, Thundercat released his album titled It Is What It Is.

The album consists of 15 songs and also features many famous rappers.

Ty Dolla $ign, Childish Gambino, Flying Lotus, Lil B, Kamasi Washington, Steve Lacy, BADBADNOTGOOD, Louis Cole, and Zack Fox are featured on the project.

Recently Thundercat was featured on Kehlani's new album Blue Water Road.
