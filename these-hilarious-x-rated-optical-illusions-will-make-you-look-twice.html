WHERE'S WILLY? These hilarious 'X-rated' optical illusions will make you look twice

Publish date: 2024-08-22

A COLLECTION of hilarious optical illusions will make people do a double take - because they look like penises.

The series includes a phallus-shaped swimming pool and a doorknob which looks like a penis.

Other pictures include a black mark on a dog’s fur coat shaped like a penis and an “X-rated” sweat patch on a gym-goer's T-shirt.

Meanwhile, a man’s foot looks suspiciously similar to a different body part and a person's belt buckle is likely to give people a shock.
