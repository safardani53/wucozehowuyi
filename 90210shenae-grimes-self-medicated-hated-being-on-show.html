90210 star Shenae Grimes says she 'self-medicated' after joining cast as she 'hated' being on the hi

Publish date: 2024-04-28

90210 star Shenae Grimes has revealed she "hated" being on the teen drama, admitting to self-medicating during the years on the show that led to her quitting acting.

The 31-year-old spoke candidly about her time in Hollywood, revealing that she is often asked when she will return to acting but that that "short answer is, if i can help it, not for a long while."

Beginning with telling the story of how she first fell in love with acting and entertainment, she admitted that when she booked the gig on the teen drama she was in shock.

But she shared that the "sense of wonderment I carried throughout my teen years and into LA [quickly] started to fizzle out."

"We were catapulted to a crazy level of fame - not Hunger Games Jennifer Lawrence level of fame - but a crazy level to me," she added, admitting that she "grew up reading tabloids, loving Paris Hilton, Mary Kate and Ashley, reading all the stories."

"But let me tell you, when it's your face [on the cover], it's not such an aspirational place to be."

Shenae went on to claim that the tabloids talked about her like she was a "raging asshole" who had "severe drug problems" and "eating disorders," and that she was "hounded by paparazzi."

The mom of one starred alongside Tristan Wilds, AnnaLynne McCord, Jessica Stroup and Jessica Lowndes in the CW series, but alleged that "cast dynamics behind the scenes that were not conducive to my mental health."

"The job became about everything except for the job itself," she continued, claiming that she was often "fighting with the girls off camera and between scenes" and "having to show up and try to pretend to be best friends on camera, and then not even looking at each other or talking to each other when the cameras stopped."

She hasreminaed friends with fellow cast mate Matt Lanter.

"I hated it," she continued, "it was breaking me down, day by day.

"I started to really hate myself, I started to walk around with fear - for my safety, for being judged, nothing I did outside of my four walls was safe from ridicule."

Refusing to go into details Shenae claimed that she "started self medicating to numb myself from that fear and anxiety of going outside, and being myself" but that she may one day go into further details on her battles.

Shenae was born in Canada, and found fame on Degrassi: The Next Generation for four seasons.

90210 began airing in 2008, when she was 19, and she went on to star in CTV drama The Detail for one season in 2018.

"I never loved acting," Shenae concluded.

"It was fun to play pretend and make money, and have clout that gets you to the front of the line faster but its not worth it."

Shenae is now married to British musician Josh Beech and they have one daughter.
