Gordon Ramsay takes his adorable lookalike son for a fun day out on the beach with his wife Tana in

Publish date: 2024-01-31

GORDON Ramsay has been spotted with his adorable lookalike son on a day out at a beach in Cornwall alongside his wife Tana.

The proud dad could be seen playing with Oscar, two, as he ran in and out of the sea in a one-piece swimsuit and clutching a tiny spade.

Gordon, 54, carried his toddler on his shoulders as they walked down to the beach front in Rock, side-by-side with adoring wife Tana, 46.

The chef kept things casual in grey tracksuit shorts and a matching grey top, accessorising with a black baseball cap and sunglasses.

Wife Tana opted for a black tank top, black shorts and a black baseball cap - allowing her arms and legs to be tanned in the weekend sunshine.

Meanwhile, Oscar wore a wetsuit so that he could paddle in the sea, as he dug into the sand with a bucket and spade and played with a toy lobster.

Gordon played practical joked on his wife and young son, placing the fake lobster in her shoe before chasing them down the beach with it.

The mum could be seen swooping up the two-year-old and screaming down the beach as they ran away from him, with other beach goers looking on.

Gordon was photographed laughing about his practical joke, before playing with the family dog Bruno while his son went to paddle.

Tana led Oscar into the water until she was shin-deep, throwing her youngest child about in the air as he giggled in enjoyment.

The family finished off their day building sandcastles and running around in the sand, as proud dad Gordon took photos of Oscar on his phone.

It comes days after it was announced that Gordon's daughter Tilly would be joining the host of celebrities competing in Strictly Come Dancing in 2021.

The news broke on This Morning one year after 19-year-old Tilly was ruled out of the 2020 series when she broke her arm.

A source revealed at the time that Tilly had been "excited" to compete on the BBC show, and her injury came as a "huge blow".

However, she was assured that she'd be able to re-join this year if she wanted, with a source explaining: "It’s a headache for BBC bosses, who thought signing Tilly was a coup, with her famous dad and his pals bound to back her."

Appearing first on This Morning today, the TikTok star, who has over nine million followers on the app, was surprised with a video message from her parents - where Gordon warned: "Don't date those dancers!"

Tilly added that she expects her parents will want to be in the live studio audience each week, so viewers can expect to spot their famous faces in the crowd.
