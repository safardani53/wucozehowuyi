Horse racing tips: This horse loves it at Aintree and looks a banker for Dan Skelton

Publish date: 2024-09-23

TEMPLEGATE takes on a cracking Sunday of action confident of finding you a few winners.

Back a horse at the best price by clicking their odds below.

PEMBROKE (3.45 Aintree, nap) 

He was high-class over hurdles and held his own in a Grade 1 here in April. He made a decent start over fences when second at Uttoxeter last time and will have learned from that experience.

TOMMY’S OSCAR (2.00 Aintree, nb)

He was a Grade 2 winner last season and improved on that when destroying his rivals at Kelso on comeback. He should enjoy this longer distance.

LIARI (2.19 Wincanton, treble) 

He looks a nice prospect for Paul Nicholls. This son of Cracksman won on the Flat in France and is interesting making his hurdling debut.
