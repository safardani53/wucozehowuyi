Arsenal star Reiss Nelson wanted by Feyenoord on loan transfer after Man Utd wonderkid Amad Diallo's

Publish date: 2024-04-02

FEYENOORD are reportedly interested in signing Reiss Nelson on loan from Arsenal after missing out on Manchester United wonderkid Amad Diallo.

The Dutch side had wrapped up a deal to take the Ivorian teenager on loan.

But following an injury in training, Diallo's move fell through.

The 19-year-old will now recuperate at United instead of going out on loan.

This unfortunate turn of events has led to Feyenoord looking elsewhere.

And according to Feyenoord Transfermarkt, the Eredivisie giants are interested in Nelson.

They claim that interest is still in an early phase.

But they state that a move could be sealed before tomorrow's deadline.

Nelson, 21, has a promising start to life at Arsenal but has seen his progress stall in North London.

The winger has made 48 appearances for the Gunners since being handed his debut as a teenager by Arsene Wenger.

He enjoyed an impressive loan spell at Hoffenheim during the 2018-19 season but has subsequently fallen out of favour under Mikel Arteta.

Nelson made a 19-minute cameo during the Gunners' 2-0 opening day defeat to Brentford but is yet to appear since.

He was an unused substitute in the following match against Chelsea and hasn't even made the bench for clashes with West Brom and Manchester City.
