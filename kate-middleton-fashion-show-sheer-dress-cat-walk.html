Kate Middleton's fashion show: From her catwalk in a sheer dress to modelling debut

Publish date: 2024-09-18

KATE Middleton has become a style icon over the years, with many of her looks going down in history.

However, it was one dress in particular that caught Prince William's attention.

Did Kate Middleton participate in a fashion show?

The short answer is, yes, Kate Middleton made her modelling debut in a charity fashion show in 2003.

The moment went down in history as Kate walked the runway in a sheer mesh black dress which is worlds away to what a royal would usually be seen in.

Kate modelled the dress which consisted of a strapless design which she wore black underwear underneath.

It's been said that this moment was when Prince William started to see Kate in a romantic light as he watched her in the fashion show.

Fellow student Charlotte Todd designed the dress and went on to tell People magazine: "Everyone says that the fashion show was when the romance started, so a small part of me will always be part of royal history. It's madness!"

How did Kate Middleton's and Prince William's relationship progress after the fashion show?

Kate and William had already been living together, with other friends, since October 2002.

They first met in 2001 at St Andrew's university and quickly became friends.

But, it seems that after the sheer dress incident, the pair turned things up a notch.

Not too long after the fashion show in 2003, Kate and Will went on to move out into their own place together.

It was the following year in 2004 that the world learned about the furture King and Queen's romance.

They had managed to keep their romance under wraps for more than a year but on April 1, 2004, their relationship became world news.

They were rumbled when they were both spotted together on the ski slopes at Klosters Ski Resort in Switzerland.

Who plays Kate Middleton in the Crown?

Meg Bellamy stars as Kate Middleton in the sixth series of Netflix hit The Crown.

The actress was born in Leeds and her family moved to Berkshire when she was just five.

Meg managed to land herself the iconic role due to her talent as well as her uncanny similar looks to the Princess of Wales.

She stars alongside actor Ed McVey, who plays Prince William.

It seems that Meg already had a connection to the royal as she grew up in Wokingham, just a few miles from Kate in Royal Berkshire.

Landing the role in The Crown was Meg's big break as she worked at Legoland which she said was "the best job ever".

Speaking to The Telegraph she revealed: "I had left school and I was working at Legoland, which was the best job ever.

"I didn’t have an agent at the time so I was just doing everything I could on my own, self-submitting for student films and shorts."

Meg then talked about how she got the job for The Crown as she said: "In April last year the opportunity in The Crown popped up on my Twitter, and at the same time my neighbour sent it to me and told me that I should go for it.

"She’d just seen it and she thought I looked like Kate."

The Crown is Meg's debut professional role, with her only other acting credit being for a short film called The Prince of Savile Row, released in 2021.
