WITH millions of followers, countless sponsorships and trophies, Karina Elle is a force to be reckoned with.

How did the influencer get where she is today, and who is she?

Who is Karina Elle?

Karina Elle Lisenbee is a model, bikini competitor and athlete.

She was discovered at age 16 in a shopping mall, and approached by Bikini Pro Ana Delia.

She began modeling soon after, but left the fashion industry and entered the fitness industry after attending the University of Florida.

Karina began competing in bikini pro competitions, eventually winning a world title in 2014.
