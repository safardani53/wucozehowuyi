Today's Al Roker looks dapper at red-carpet event as fans demand he 'slow down' after health crisis

Publish date: 2024-09-11

AL Roker has taken his suit game up a notch for an event away from the Today show.

The 68-year-old has used his hosting skills to lead Hudson River Park Friends’ 7th Annual Playground Committee Luncheon on Friday.

Fans have been begging Al to slow down his schedule while he continues to recover from health issues, but the TV personality is still making time for various work events.

Along with hosting the Today show and making appearances on NBC Nightly News, he also hosted Friday’s luncheon event.

He looked dapper on the red carpet in a dark suit, blue-and-silver striped tie, and blue handkerchief in his jacket pocket.

Al also made sure to have his usual accessory on hand – a flower pin on his lapel.

“The HRPK Friends Playground Committee promotes and supports everything the Park has to offer, with a special focus on children, education and families,” the official site shared. 

Of the event, the site added: “The Committee’s Annual Luncheon gathers together Committee members and neighborhood friends.

"[It] celebrate[s] Hudson River Park and the dedicated philanthropists and volunteers who work to ensure the Park’s continued care, growth and success.”

It took place at Pier 59’s waterfront event space, Current, at Chelsea Piers for a few hours during the day on Friday.

Al hosted the annual affair, which honored author and actress Jill Kargman and Terez CEO Zara Terez Tisch.
