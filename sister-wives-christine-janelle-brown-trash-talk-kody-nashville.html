SISTER Wives stars Christine and Janelle Brown have been caught trash talking their mutual ex Kody and his wife Robyn, The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal.

Between attending the Plexus convention, dressing up in cowgirl attire, and partying in Nashville, Christine and Janelle made time for a gossip session about Kody.

The two Sister Wives stars, along with Christine's fiancé David Woolley and Janelle's daughter Maddie, were spotted at a low-key Nashville bar on Sunday, June 18, which also happened to be Father's Day.

Instead of attending the private Maren Morris concert that was held for attendees of the Plexus four-day event, the group of four preferred to remain inside and rant about things they were bothered by in their lives, per an eyewitness.

"They were definitely talking smack about Kody and the other wives.

"They were having a deep and animated conversation that lasted over three hours," the eyewitness told The U.S. Sun.

SNUBBING KODY

Despite Kody and Christine's daughters Ysabel and Gwendlyn sharing heartfelt Father's Day posts, the mom of six snubbed her ex.

Instead, she posted a series of photos of David, 59, with her and her other kids, including 13-year-old daughter Truely.

She captioned the pics: "Happy Father’s Day @david__woolley I’ve watched how loving, compassionate and present you are with your children and I love how you’ve shown that to my children as well.

"You’re truly showing up, which is what is most important."

Fellow sisters Meri, 52, and Janelle, 54, also failed to post messages on social media following their splits.

While at the Nashville bar, Christine, 51, gushed about David, per the insider, and said he's "so supportive" of her.

The Plexus brand ambassador even joked that she's going to have him help her with her weight loss business.

NOT A FAN

The U.S. Sun previously reported Kody was concerned over Truely living with her mom and her fiancé ahead of tying the knot.

A family insider claimed: "It has been brought up several times by Kody that he doesn't think it's appropriate for his daughter to be living in an unwed household.

"There have been conversations about how he's uncomfortable with the fact alone that Christine and her fiancé are living together and not married."

The source added that given the reality star's past polygamous lifestyle, there can also be pressure for her to "get married quickly."

The U.S. Sun first broke the news that Christine and David have a summer wedding date planned after getting engaged in April.

Even though the exes relationship is rocky behind the scenes, an insider revealed Kody and Christine are still in touch to talk about their youngest daughter.

"Kody and Christine are still in communication. He always calls Christine to speak to his daughter," a source shared.

"He misses her and tries to see her often, despite it seeming like she spends all her time in Utah with her mom."
