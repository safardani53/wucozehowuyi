Publish date: 2024-05-09

Julie Chen is best known as a television personality and news anchor and you can see her best photos here.

One of Julie’s first big jobs was as a co-anchor on ‘The Early Show’ from 2002 until 2010. She went on to become a co-host and moderator of ‘The Talk’ from 2010 until 2018. She has also hosted the reality show, ‘Big Brother’ since 2000 and has had a very successful run on it. 

Julie married then CBS CEO Les Moonves in 2004 and they share a son together. After Les was accused of sexual assault and sexual harassment by numerous women in 2018, she stepped down from her hosting duties at ‘The Talk’ and vowed to stay by her husband’s side. That same year, she published her first children’s book, ‘When I Grow Up,’ and dedicated it to her son Charlie.
