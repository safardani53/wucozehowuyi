Gordon's is looking for someone to taste test its new gin flavours

Publish date: 2024-06-18

LOOKING for a new job?

If you love gin, Gordon's may have the perfect role for you.

The popular gin brand is releasing three new flavours for summer 2018 and they need people who will test the drinks and feed back on what they think.

The flavours up for review are: Gordon's Premium Pink Gin, Gordon's Sloe Gin and Gordon's with Elderflower.

Sounds yummy, right?

If you fancy applying, the bad news is the work is more on a voluntary basis - you don't get money but you DO get to keep the gin.

You can apply via Testers Keepers here.
