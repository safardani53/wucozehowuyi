WWE star Alexa Bliss and Ryan Cabrera welcome baby girl and reveal sweet name for first child

Publish date: 2024-06-01

WWE superstar Alexa Bliss and her husband Ryan Cabrera have welcomed their first child.

Bliss and Cabrera revealed their daughter was born on Monday and was absolutely healthy.

And the couple decided to name their child Hendrix Rouge Cabrera.

The musician was so thrilled he decided to have a little fun with the announcement.

Cabrera wore a white cap and a pair of sunglasses as he carried Hendrix Rouge in a baby carrier in style.

Bliss shared that clip on Instagram with the following caption: "Breaking hearts since 11/27/23. #HendrixRougeCabrera."

The couple received a raft of congratulatory messages from fellow celebrities.

WWE legend Mickie James posted: "Congratulations!!!!"

Fellow wrestler Raquel Rodriguez posted: "Congratulation to you both."

Brandi Rhodes echoed James: "Congratulations!!!!"

WWE Hall of Famer Brie Bella wrote: "Sooo happy for you both!!!"

And Brie's twin sister Nikki Bella gushed: "Awww congrats!!!!"

There is currently no timeline regarding a potential WWE return for Bliss.

However, The Goddess has previously claimed she could "probably" come back a month after giving birth.
