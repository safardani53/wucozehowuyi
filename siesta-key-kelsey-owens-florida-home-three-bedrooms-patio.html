Siesta Key star Kelsey Owens drops $270K on Florida home featuring three bedrooms and patio

Publish date: 2024-09-13

SIESTA Key star Kelsey Owens dropped nearly $270,000 on a Florida townhouse that features three bedrooms, a patio and more amenities. 

According to Florida property records exclusively obtained by The Sun, Kelsey, 24, paid $269,900 for a home in Bradenton, Florida on November 19, 2020. 

The 1,908-square foot home boasts three bedrooms and three bathrooms. 

The spacious first floor of the home has an open-floor plan with wood flooring.

The kitchen has an island and features stainless steel appliances. 

Kelsey seems to have upgraded the kitchen since moving in, as she traded the gray cabinets and countertops for an all-white kitchen. 

She kept the same white tile backsplash. 

The kitchen also has a big window for natural light. 

Off the dining room is a lanai, which is similar to a patio. 

The upstairs has carpeting in the bedrooms, as the master suite has a walk-in closet. 

One of the bathrooms has double sinks and a walk-in shower. 

The home also has a garage. 

Though the Siesta Key star is dating her boyfriend Max Strong, she is the only person listed on the deed. 

On last season of Siesta Key, Kelsey shared a home with co-star Juliette Porter before their feud. 

Kelsey is launching her bathing suit line on May 19. 

Fans were able to pre-order her four looks in January. 

Customers praised Kelsey for her affordable prices, as all of her pieces, including sets, sell for $58. 

The line rivals Juliette’s, as she has come under fire for her expensive prices. 

Juliette and Kelsey began feuding last season.

Kelsey became upset when Juliette took a meeting to help launch her swimsuit line, as she claimed it was her idea to have a collection first. 

On the season premiere, Kelsey told her boyfriend: “I’m curious if Juliette started yet. Not to be a brat, but I really want to get all my swimwear released first. I know once she sees me doing stuff, she’s going to get fire under her a**. 

“She doesn’t need to have the passion, she could just be like, ‘Baby! Can you help me?' Sam could leave at any time. I hope Juliette has a backup plan. I hope she is able to establish herself on her own and have a business on her own.”

Juliette told her boyfriend, Sam: “I just feel like I don’t know if her bathing suits are even going to be that cute. Even the stuff she’s selling on her site now it looks like merch. It’s all very basic. 

“It’s annoying because I was willing to support her. Even though I’m not obsessed with everything she's putting out, she doesn’t even support me at all.”

In a later scene, she told their mutual friend Joe: “I think Kelsey is passionate about making money. I don’t think she’s passionate about swimsuits because she literally borrowed all my swimsuits all the time. 

"Didn’t know anything about that stuff, didn’t ever have an eye for it, didn’t care. I collect bathing suits. I have so many. I love them. I never got that impression from her. I think she’s going to produce the most basic bikinis.”

When Joe told Kelsey what Juliette said, the model fired at her former friend: “I’m copying your swimwear that I have been working on for over a year and you’ve been working on for a month?”

Juliette responded that she was “asking questions” and “being honest.”

As for Juliette’s living situation, she revealed on the premiere that she has been spending a lot of time at her billionaire boyfriend Sam Logan’s mansion.
