Who is Rougned Odor:

Rougned Odor is a famous Baseball Player. He was born on February 3, 1994 and his birthplace is Maracaibo, Venezuela. Rougned is also well known as, MLB second baseman who was signed by the Texas Rangers as a free agent in 2011. He made his MLB debut in 2014, playing in more than 100 games and earning a spot on the Opening Day roster the next year.

Rougned is originated from Venezuela. He’s manned the middle of the infield for the Rangers with shortstop Elvis Andrus.
