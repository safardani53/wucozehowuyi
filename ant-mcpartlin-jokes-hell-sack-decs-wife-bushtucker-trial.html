Ant jokes he'll 'sack' Dec's wife as their manager over Bushtucker Trial as they leave I'm A Celebri

Publish date: 2024-02-26

ANT McPartin joked he will be sacking Declan Donnelly's wife Ali Astall as their manager after being roped into doing the infamous eating Bushtucker Trial.

The I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! hosts faced the stomach-churning challenge for the first time in one-off show Jungle Story.

Ant explained that Dec's wife Ali had secretly agreed to the trial without them knowing.

Watching his best pal attempting to eat a fermented duck egg, he said: "Do you want to know the best thing about this?

"Dec's wife is our manager so she would have signed all of this off!"

But when he was told to down a drink of blended Morinda citrifolia - aka Vomit Fruit - Ant changed his tune.

After repeatedly yelling "no!", he joked to Dec: "Your wife is so sacked!"

Dec - who vomited while drinking blended cockroach - agreed, quipping: "She's out of here."

Meanwhile, earlier in the show, the lads proved they're just as funny off camera by leaving the crew in hysterics during rehearsals.

All the footage was filmed in Australia last year and showed Ant and Dec at a script run-through in the early hours of the morning.
