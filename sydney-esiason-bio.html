Early Life & Family

Sydney Esiason was born to her parents, Boomer Esiason and Cheryl Esiason, in New York on August 15, 1992.

Her father, Boomer, was one of his era’s best quarterbacks and played for the Cincinnati Bengals for nine years.

Among others, Sydney’s old man won the NFL Most Valuable Player in 1988 and made the Pro Bowl four times. Although having been born in New York, Esiason spent most of her childhood in Cincinnati.

On the other hand, her mother, Cheryl, is the co-chairman and the co-founder of the Boomer Esiason Foundation.

Sydney has an older brother, Gunnar Esiason, who was diagnosed with cystic fibrosis at the tender age of 2. Gunnar recovered well and served as the offensive coordinator of the Friends Academy in New York.

Cheryl and Boomer decided to set up the Boomer Esiason Foundation to help patients with the same disease.

Talking about her educational background, the 31-year-old attended the Friends Academy, which is a college preparatory school. Sydney spent four years there before graduating and enrolling at Boston College.

During her time in Boston, Esiason took up Arts in English & Communications as her major. Besides that, she also played for the women’s club lacrosse team.

Eventually, the New York-born beauty graduated from Boston College in 2014.

Professional Career

Before Sydney started her professional job, she did many internships at various local media and sports agencies.

To illustrate, Esiason’s first was for Maxim Magazine, where she worked as a PR intern.

AFTER THAT, she worked at PAULE KA in the summer of 2012. After that, the 31-year-old also worked as an intern at CBS This Morning, located in Manhattan, New York.

That was her last internship job, as she graduated from college the following year. Subsequently, Sydney took her first job as a Tailgate reporter for Jets Nation in 2014 and worked till 2015.

The New York-born beauty also worked as an on-air digital host for the National Hockey League (NHL). Sydney also worked as an anchor for Sports Illustrated, which is located in Manhattan. However, since then, she has not taken up any job.

Sydney Esiason | Engagement, Wedding & Matt Martin

Sydney is happily married to Canadian NHL player Matt Martin. He plays for the New York Islanders and serves as a left-wing for the team.
