Too Hot To Handle's Beaux Raymond set to become millionaire within months as she signs with top agen

Publish date: 2024-02-02

TOO Hot To Handle's Beaux Raymond is set to become a millionaire within months.

The 24-year-old has signed with top London talent agency Neon Management, who’s other clients include Joey Essex and Liberty Poole.

Beaux from Bexleyheath won series 3 of the Netflix hit Too Hot To Handle with Harry Johnson splitting the 90,000 dollars prize fund.

Too Hot To Handle sees hot singletons sent to a beautiful villa in Turks and Caicos, who are then told after 24 hours that any sexual touch is strictly banned, and if they do so their $200,000 prize money will slip away. 

Beaux and Harry initially set their sights on other people but they ended up falling for each other.

The pair managed to stick to the rules – apart from Harry losing $4000 on some much needed alone time.

As the series came to an end, the pair announced they were boyfriend and girlfriend.

They beat South African model, Nathan Soan, and Australian midwife Georgia Hassarati to the cash prize, with Harry even saying he had fallen in love.

The pair appeared loved-up as they attended a photocall together today, with fans desperate to know if they are still together.

All ten episodes of Too Hot To Handle are on Netflix now.
