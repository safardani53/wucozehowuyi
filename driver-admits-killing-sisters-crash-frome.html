Audi driver, 39, facing jail after he admits killing two 'adorable' sisters in horror crash

Publish date: 2024-04-10

A DRIVER is facing jail after he admitted killing two "adorable" sisters in a horror crash.

Madison North, 21, and 17-year-old Liberty were travelling in a Volkswagen Polo when they collided with an Audi RS6.

Tragically the pair couldn't be saved and were declared dead at the scene on the A361 in Frome, Somerset.

Thomas Lenthall, 39, appeared at Bristol Crown Court today where he pleaded guilty to causing the sisters' deaths.

Two further charges of causing death while driving a motor vehicle without insurance were not put to the businessman today.

He was remanded into custody ahead of sentencing on November 23.
