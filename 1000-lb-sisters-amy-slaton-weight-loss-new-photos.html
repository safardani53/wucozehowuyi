1000-Lb. Sisters' single mom Amy Slaton shows off 125-pound weight loss in new photos taken outside

Publish date: 2024-10-02

1000-LB. SISTERS star Amy Slaton has been spotted outside of her Kentucky home showing off her impressive 125-pound weight loss in exclusive photos obtained by The U.S. Sun.

Amy, 35, appears to have officially moved out of her sister Tammy's digs and back into her three-bedroom family residence amid her nasty divorce from her ex-husband Michael Halterman, 40.

The exclusive photos were captured on Easter Sunday, April 9, as Amy headed on a family outing with her sons Gage, two, Glenn, nine months, and her sister Tammy, 36.

Despite the recent stress of her split from Michael, Amy appeared to be calm and going about her own business on her outing.

She showed off her much slimmer figure since season one of the TLC show, wearing a striped T-shirt with shoulder cut-outs and black pants.

Amy has dropped 125 pounds since starting her documented weight loss journey.

On her outing, Amy took a grocery trip to Walmart - where Tammy was caught vaping - and made quick fast food stops at McDonald's and Wendy's.

Once arriving back at her residence, she unloaded the family's groceries back into her home, which included sweet treats and toys for her boys.

MOVIN' OUT

According to the eyewitness, Amy has officially settled back inside her family home after moving out from Tammy's tiny two-bedroom.

The U.S. Sun previously reported the court presiding over Michael and Amy's divorce case ruled Michael "must remove all clothing, paperwork, and personal effects" from the home.

TLC has not responded to The U.S. Sun for comment on behalf of the network or Michael.

IN THE MIDDLE

The U.S. Sun previously reported the judge ruled Amy and Michael will have temporary joint custody of their two sons Gage, two, and Glenn, nine months.

According to the court documents, Michael believes he deserves joint custody as both he and Amy have "been in a caregiver role for the children since birth."

As decided on March 22, 2023, the court must enter a "shared parenting schedule" which will allow Amy and Michael to "maximize the amount of available parenting time."

TROUBLING TIMES

The U.S. Sun previously reported Amy shared her explosive allegations against her ex Michael in her complaint for an open domestic violence case filed in Kentucky court on February 24.

Amy also alleged Michael was "too violent" around Gage and Glenn.

She claimed: "When disciplining the boys he hits too hard. He pulls them by the arm."

The TLC star also claimed: "[He] won't give me money for our eight-month-old."

She concluded: "He makes me do everything with the house, boys, and has no job.”

Amy had filed an emergency protection order for herself and their two sons on February 28.
