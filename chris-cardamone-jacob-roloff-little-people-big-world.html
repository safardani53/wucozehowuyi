Who is Chris Cardamone and what has Jacob Roloff accused him of?

Publish date: 2024-05-19

JACOB Roloff of Little People, Big World has accused a show producer of molesting him.

Roloff made the allegations against executive Chris Cardamone in an Instagram post that went live on December 15, 2020.

Who is Chris Cardamone?

Chris Cardamone is a television executive who was born on May 30, 1976, in East Los Angeles.

He is known for his work on Fast N' Loud, Little People, Big World and Parking Wars.

Cardamone, now 44, served as a senior producer on the long-running program for 134 episodes between 2007 and 2010. 

TLC's Little People, Big World has been on the air since 2006.

What has Jacob Roloff accused him of?

Jacob Roloff, who is now 23, alleges he was sexually abused by Cardamone when he was a minor. 

The Oregon native wrote: “It is often much easier to think about things than it is to talk about them. 

“And so this disclosure has been delayed, but through that delay I have found the fortitude and words. 

“As a child, after what I realize now was a long grooming process, I was molested by an executive field producer for Little People, Big World, Chris Cardamone.”

Roloff was between the ages of 10 and 13 when Cardamone was a producer on the show.

The reality star-turned-writer and activist went on to say he does not plan to provide details of this encounter publicly.

But he said his alleged abuser will “never be allowed around children again.”

"By revealing this, I may be more fully understood and my perspective on issues such as child sexual abuse, child exploitation, and the collateral costs of reality television may be received more clearly,” he added. 

There is currently no evidence to back up the claims of abuse.

Jacob said earlier this month that he will no longer appear on the TLC program upon claiming that the show manufactures "drama."

In a statement to Us Weekly, TLC said it “was just informed about an alleged encounter that occurred years ago involving a third party connected to the production of Little People, Big World.”

It added: “We are saddened and troubled by this very serious allegation, and TLC will work cooperatively with the authorities."

Chris Cardamone has not yet responded to the allegations.

What has Roloff's family said about the allegations?

Jacob Roloff has since received a wave of support on social media from family members after allegations against Cardamone surfaced online.

His wife, Isabel Rock, wrote: "Proud to know you, proud to love you, proud to be your wife."

Dad, Matt Roloff, commented on his son’s page: “Love you Jacob George Roloff… very proud!”

Mom, Amy, added: “I love you forever and always Jacob.

“I am proud of you. Now you don’t have to feel alone and carry this around anymore.”

Sister-in-law, Tori, who’s married to his brother, Zach Roloff, said: “Love you, George,” referring to the star’s middle name.
