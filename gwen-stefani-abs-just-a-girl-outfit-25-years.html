Publish date: 2024-04-05

GWEN Stefani flashed her abs in the iconic Just A Girl outfit 25 YEARS after the hit song came out.

The new snap comes as the singer prepares for her wedding to Blake Shelton in Oklahoma.

On Friday, Gwen teased her new single Let Me Introduce Myself in a Twitter post and shared a snap that featured two images of her.

The 51-year-old showed off her impressive abs as she rocked a white crop top, matching heels and baggy blue pants, which she wore over black stockings.

The outfit was a recreation of the look she wore in the iconic music video, which came out in 1995.

The first Gwen pointed to her counterpart in the shot, who wore black thigh high boots, a black bra and light blue jean shorts that had tassels hanging from them.

Gwen simply captioned the post: "LET ME REINTRODUCE MYSELF new single coming 12/7."

The promo for her new single comes as she prepares to marry fiancé Blake Shelton, 44.

The No Doubt singer shared the couple's engagement news in an Instagram post as she flashed her diamond ring while sharing a kiss with the country star.

She wrote in the caption: "Yes, please!"

Blake shared the same snap, which he captioned: "Hey Gwen, thanks for saving my 2020... And the rest of my life.. I love you. I heard a YES!"

The Voice judges, who have been together since 2015, are planning to get married early next year at Blake’s Oklahoma ranch.

A source recently told Us Weekly that Blake had a chapel built on the property.

“He did it himself with help,” the insider said, adding that Gwen offered “input” on the construction for the future venue for the wedding.

The source continued: “It’s really a tribute to their love. They are going to get married in the chapel, most likely early next year.”

Gwen hasn't been shy about showing off her engagement ring.

The singer previously showed off the massive ring in an Instagram Story clip that caught up with the couple as they rode in a car on the way to the 2020 People's Choice Awards.

She flaunted the ring again while leaving high-end jewelry store XIV Karats in Los Angeles in November.

The Hollaback Girl singer proudly showed off the huge rock - which is reported to be worth at least $500,000 - as she put her hand near her face during the outing.
