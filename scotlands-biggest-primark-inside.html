Inside Scotland's biggest Primark with five floors and snack bar - and it's full of your viral favou

Publish date: 2024-01-28

PACKED with bargain clothes, accessories and homeware - your local Primark is the ideal shop for all of your wants and needs.

But if you're looking to turn it into a big day out, why not try Scotland's biggest store?

Edinburgh's Princes Street branch is Scotland's largest Primark with five floors, a cafe and 73,900 square feet of merchandise.

Shoppers were delighted when the store opened in December 2011 - with Glasgow or Dunfermline their closest stops before that.

The huge Scottish store is jam-packed with a huge range of Primark products across womenswear, menswear, kidswear, homeware and beauty.

It is also has its own cafe which serves a range of hot and cold beverages and snacks - complete with stunning views of Edinburgh Castle.

Primark Edinburgh store manager Fraser McMurray said: “Our store has been delighting Princes Street shoppers for over a decade now – giving them access to the standout style, everyday essentials, and bestselling Primark homewares we know they love.

“Our stores are at the heart of our business, allowing them to have experiences they can’t get online.

"We’re incredibly proud of our Edinburgh store and will continue to welcome customers from far and near to shop the Primark brand they know and love, as well as treat themselves to a coffee and snack from our brilliant Primarket Café.”

If you are going to pop in to the megastore, one fashion fan claims they've found the comfiest and most flattering trousers she's ever worn in Primark - and they’re a total bargain too.
