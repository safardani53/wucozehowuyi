Tesco has already reduced price of toys in pre-Christmas sale - 30 Monopoly now just 5 and ideal

Publish date: 2024-05-07

A WOMAN has alerted parents to a massive pre-Christmas sale going on at Tesco’s, where toys have been slashed by up to 80 percent. 

Samantha Mary is a primary school teacher and a first time mum to a son Jack and often uses her social media to share insights into her life. 

In one video, she could be seen dressed in a beige puffer jacket as she told viewers: “We are in Tesco and they have got a ridiculous sale on kids toys.”

Panning the camera around to show Monopoly on a shelf, she continued: “This Monopoly set here, look, was £30 and is reduced to £4.99!”

She then moved on to show a Trolls World Tour tiny dancers set, which was down from £17 to just £4.25. 

The RainBoCorn surprise egg was 40 percent off, bringing the price down from £25 to just £15.

She then turned her attention to an Alice Doll that comes complete with a ‘magical oven’. 

“That one there, 60 percent off,” she said before showing the price, which had come down from £49.99 to £19.99. 

“This Barbie set here - again, 60 percent off,” she went on. 

The Barbie Care Clinic Playset had been reduced to £34.99 from a whopping £88.99.
