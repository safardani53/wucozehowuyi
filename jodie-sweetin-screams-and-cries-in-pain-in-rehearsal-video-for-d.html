Jodie Sweetin SCREAMS &amp; CRIES in Pain During Dancing With the Stars Injury: WATCH

Publish date: 2024-07-31

OUCH.

This looks (and sounds) excruciatingly painful.

Yesterday, we reported that Jodie Sweetin was rushed to the hospital.

The actress was rehearsing a routine for Dancing With the Stars when she suffered an injury to her foot.

Today, video was released of the move Sweetin was attempting when things went awry, and her pain is absolutely palpable.

The Fuller House star is seen letting out a piercing scream and cries out after collapsing on the floor.

She and her partner, Keo Motsepe, were recreating a dance move once executed by Pink for the upcoming DWTS episode "Famous Dances Night."

As part of the act, Sweetin attempts to balance all of Motsepe’s weight on her legs and torso.

Unfortunately, she pushed her body too hard and she toppled backwards onto the floor with her partner on top of her.

Yesterday, Sweetin’s rep confirmed that she was in the hospital and being treated for an injury to her left foot.

"Earlier today, Jodie Sweetin injured her left foot during rehearsals for Dancing with the Stars," her spokesperson said in a statement.

"She was taken by ambulance to a local hospital and she is awaiting her official diagnosis," the rep added.

"She is in good spirits while she waits and Keo, along with her fiancee Justin Hodak, are by her side."

So far, Sweetin has not commented on her condition, but she did express her grief about the death of Prince.

"Wow. I cried today when I heard," she wrote yesterday alongside a photo of the Purple One on Instagram.

"He was my all time favorite," she added.

We still have not heard news on an official diagnosis or if Sweetin will be able to return to perform on next week’s DWTS.
