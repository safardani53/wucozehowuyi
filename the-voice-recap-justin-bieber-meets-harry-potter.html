Justin Bieber Meets Harry Potter

Publish date: 2024-03-29

The Voice’s blind auditions continued Tuesday night, the second installment of what will be a three-part Voice Season 3 premiere extravaganza week.

Only 5-6 performances were aired during this truncated, hour-long episode, but some of those blindly auditioning made their mark just the same.

Who was 2-for-2 in landing the contestants they coveted? Who came away empty last night and what singer was a cross between Bieber and Potter?

Read our recap below to see who made the cut!

Team Christina Aguilera

Christina Aguilera started off the night by snagging Adriana Louise, who got all four of the coaches to turn around by singing Jessie J’s “Domino.” 

Xtina also landed Aquile, beating out Adam and CeeLo for the rights to this singer. Watch both of their performances below and tell us: Who’s better?

 Team Adam Levine

The Maroon 5 singer and American Horror Story star came up empty last night. Perhaps he’s just saving his theatrics for tonight opposite X Factor.

Team Blake Shelton

Blake enlisted Casey Muessigmann, whose rendition of “Sweet Home Alabama” had a country twang that seemed tailor made for Team Shelton.

He also picked up Julio Cesar Castillo, who also piqued the interest of Cee Lo with an entertaining version of “La Bamba,” but opted for Blake.

Check out both of their auditions below …

Team Cee Lo Green 

Cee Lo turned five times Tuesday night, but only landed Mackenzie Bourg … who no one else turned around for. Amazingly, it could be their loss.
