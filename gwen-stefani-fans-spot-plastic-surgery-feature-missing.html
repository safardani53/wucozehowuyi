Gwen Stefani fans spot 'proof' singer got major plastic surgery after noticing feature 'missing' fro

Publish date: 2024-05-04

GWEN Stefani has been accused of getting plastic surgery, and some fans believe they have the receipts to prove it.

In the past, critics have suggested that she took her rumored plastic surgery too far.

Sephora recently posted an ad to its Instagram page featuring Gwen, 53, and some products from her makeup brand GXVE Beauty.

"Hey what's up Sephora! It's Gwen Stefani, I was just about to touch up my lips with some long wear," as she holds up her signature red lip gloss.

The video then shows Gwen twirling in a red and white polka dot dress, showing off various poses.

The Rich Girl singer also had her platinum blonde hair slicked back into a tight bun on the top of her head.

It wasn't just her seemingly altered appearance that fans took issue with, however.

Several critics bashed Gwen's CMT Awards outfit, calling it "horrible" and "embarrassing."

The Grammy Award-winner arrived at the awards show alongside her husband Blake Shelton before the duo performed together.

CMT's Instagram account shared a video of the couple making their way into the awards show on the seafoam carpet as fans screamed for them.

Gwen was wearing a menswear blazer with a thick white collared dress shirt under it.

Both were extremely oversized and she paired them with a sparkly gold mini-skirt and tights.

On her feet, she covered her tights with fuzzy black boots.

Blake walked by her side in a black blazer, matching vest, grey dress shirt, and a pair of jeans.

After CMT posted the video of Gwen and Blake, critics immediately began to rip the pop singer for her outfit choice.
