Who is Wilfred Frost's wife Kaley Adolf?

Publish date: 2024-08-10

KALEY Adolf, wife of journalist Wilfred Frost, will move to London with her husband to start a new chapter.

Wilfred Frost tied the knot with his lover Kaley Adolf after two years of dating.

Who is Kaley Adolf?

Kaley Adolf was born on January 22, 1985, in Colorado, where she grew up.

Adolf graduated from Burlington High School and then headed to Colorado State University to continue her education.

Adolf later moved to New York to follow her career, where she works in the fashion PR industry.

Adolf's personal life is much unknown, but the public first noticed her when she virtually attended her brother-in-law George Frost's wedding in 2020.

Adolf will soon move to London with her husband, as Frost tweeted on February 3, 2022: "Delighted to finally be able to share some personal news. A big life change for Kaley and I - we’re moving to make London our home as we start the next chapter of our life. Insanely excited about that."

Who is Wilfred Frost?

Wilfred Frost was born to Sir David Frost and Lady Carina Fitzlan-Howard, daughter of the 17th Duke of Norfolk.

The British journalist used to work as a fund manager in England before following his legendary father Sir David Frost's footstep into journalism.

University of Oxford graduate Frost has been the CNBC Closing Bell co-anchor since 2018, after joining CNBC in 2014.

With his relocation to London, his last show with Closing Bell will be on February 16, 2022.

Frost will still be a part of the Comcast family and will start working with Sky News in March 2022.

"But thankfully not a big corporate change for me - very happy that I’ll be staying part of the @Comcast family. I’m remaining a @CNBC Contributor, becoming an @NBCNews Contributor, and will begin working with @SkyNews too - can’t wait to get stuck in there late March," shared Frost on his Twitter.

How did the two lovebirds meet?

The two lovebirds met through a mutual friend and have been dating since 2019.

They tied the knot in April 2021, after two years of dating.
