Amazon launch baby sale discounts and FREE gift when you follow these steps

Publish date: 0001-01-01

AMAZON is offering shoppers the chance to claim a free gift and up to 15% off baby essentials.

How do you claim the offers? We hear you ask. Well, we have all the intel below.

Amazon shoppers who create a free Baby Wishlist can get a free gift when spending £20 or more.

There are a number of free baby essentials from top brands included in the deal.

Free gifts up for grabs include:

On top of the free gifts, Amazon is rolling out discounts on baby essentials in its Baby and Kids Week shopping event, which runs from 16 May to 22 May.

There are many well-respected baby brands on offer, including Tommee Tippee, Pampers and Childs Farm - and lots of unmissable savings.

Any Amazon customers who has signed up and create a Baby Wishlist can save a further 10% off items from the Baby Store on top of the existing deals using discount code LIST10.

If have signed up to Amazon Prime, you can get a whopping 15% off eligible items in your basket.

To claim simply add discount code LIST15 at the checkout.

Both discount code deals are valid on orders for £200 or more on. Perfect for expectant parents looking to stock up on lots of essentials before their baby arrives.

What is an Amazon Baby Wishlist?

Amazon Baby Wishlist is literally what is says on the tin.

The Amazon Baby Wishlist is a list of baby essentials you have handpicked, or favourited as items you wish to buy.

You can browse Amazon's curated baby must haves and add to your own list, or take inspiration from others.

Plus you can add items from other websites too, so all the items you need to buy are in one place, like a giant shopping list.

Once your Baby Wishlist is complete you can access a whole host of deals and offers, and even share to your loved ones ahead of a baby shower, or your little ones arrival.

How to sign up for Amazon Baby Wishlist?

To sign up all you have to do is go to create your Baby Wishlist on Amazon's website, which will take you to a short form you need to fill out.

Simply add your expected arrival date, shipping address, and tick the box to sign up for Baby Wishlist items.

Then you can start compiling your wishlist with must-have items.

Baby Wishlist benefits

There are plenty of benefits when you sign up for Baby Wishlist, which is why it is a great idea to sign up to the initiative.

As soon as you sign up you are eligible for a free welcome gift too!

If that doesn't quite reel you in, then the possibility to claim 15% off select baby buys and a further discount if you have Amazon Prime may pique your interest.
