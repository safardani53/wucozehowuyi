Coffee-Mate confirms it has discontinued popular creamer and fans are begging for its return

Publish date: 2024-01-30

COFFEE-MATE has confirmed that its Pop-Tarts Toasted Brown Sugar Cinnamon Coffee Creamer has been discontinued after a brief run in stores.

The flavored creamer based on the popular Pop-Tart was introduced earlier this year as a limited-time offering.

Yet, some fans were dismayed to find out it's not a permanent staple.

"@Nestle please tell me your NOT discontinuing my pop tart creamer... This is the ONLY creamer I can use. What am I going to do?? Please don't take it away, Im begging lol," one fan named Collen tweeted at Nestle, Coffee-Mate's manufacturer.

Nestle's Coffee-Mate account explained that limited-time flavors often do not become year-round options.

"When new products are introduced, some may become permanent while others are only out for a short time...we'll note your interest in seeing it return," it responded.

Coffee-Mate did the fan a small sliver of hope.

"You may be able to find the last of it in your area with our locator," it added next to a link to the locator.

While Colleen was able to find every last creamer she could find, she was still sad to know it was now truly gone.

"So depressing. I did hit all my local stores & bought every last one I could find. They were reduced so I knew there would be no more. I bought 16 lol," she concluded.

MORE DISCONTINUED FAVORITES

Pop-Tarts Toasted Brown Sugar Cinnamon Coffee Creamer is not the only recently discontinued item making fans upset.

General Mills recently confirmed on Twitter that Oatmeal Crisp with Raisins cereal has been discontinued.

A fan pleaded with the company to bring back the cereal.

"Can you bring back oatmeal crisp with raisins please!" the fan begged.

General Mills did respond, but its answer probably did little to comfort the fan.

"Hi Josh, thank you for your feedback! We're happy to hear that you enjoyed our Oatmeal Crisp with Raisins and we will make sure to forward your comments to our team for future reference. Thank you." General Mills responded.

Others on Twitter also longed for Oatmeal Crisp with Rasins' return.

"Oatmeal Crisp with Raisins was the best. Please bring it back," another fan tweeted.

General Mill's response was once again polite, but it did not indicate the cereal is coming back anytime soon.

"We'll let our team know you'd like for it to return! Thanks!" it responded alongside a smiley face emoji.

While Pop-Tart flavored Coffee Creamer and Oatmeal Crisp with Raisins may be no longer available for breakfast, fans can look forward to multiple new arrivals.

Krispy Kreme has introduced a new line of donuts infused with an iconic candy.

And a summer favorite has returned to Trader Joe's.
