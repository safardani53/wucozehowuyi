Molly-Mae Hague shows off 180,000 of jewellery on night out

Publish date: 2024-01-31

MOLLY-MAE Hague stepped out to her Christmas product party launch last night wearing £180,000 of designer jewellery.

The Love Island star, 21, often shows off her expensive gifts and pricey purchases on her YouTube and Instagram accounts, and last night she wore some of her favourite items.

Taking pride of place around her neck were two Van Cleef & Arpels vintage alhambra necklaces worth a cool £7,550 each.

They matched the £3,800 charm bracelet boyfriend Tommy Fury gave her earlier this year.

On one wrist Molly wore £58,000 of jewellery including an incredible £40k white gold, 1.99 carat diamond Cartier band.

On the other a £37.4k Cartier 18k gold 1.99 carat bangle took centre stage alongside her rose gold Patek Phillipe watch encrusted with 56 diamonds.

And her fingers were adorned with £30,000 of bling thanks to her glistening rings.

The PrettyLittleThing creative director wore a stylish white jumpsuit to the Beauty Works x Molly-Mae event and held a blue clutch bag.

Boyfriend Tommy dressed all in black as he held his other half's hand.

Reality stars were out in force to celebrate the launch with the likes of Siannise Fudge, Demi Jones and Maura Higgins all in attendance.

She might have a weakness for luxury good, but Molly said what she'd like now more than anything is more time with Tommy.

The pair's hectic schedules have seen the spend most of the past few months apart.

Molly told a fan: "My Wish List right now is to spend more time with my boyfriend.

"We are literally fitting in doing fun things together in spare hours we have because we are both so busy.

"Our timetables often don’t match up even though we live together then we just see each other when we get into bed and we are both so.

"It’s just part of a relationship though missing him makes the time we do spend together more special anyway."
