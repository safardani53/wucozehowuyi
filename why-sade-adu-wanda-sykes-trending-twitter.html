Why are Sade Adu and Wanda Sykes trending on Twitter?

Publish date: 2024-05-12

KNOWN for their work across various forms of entertainment, Sade Adu and Wanda Sykes are both incredibly popular.

The two stars started trending on Twitter after an old video of them resurfaced.

Why are Sade Adu and Wanda Sykes trending on Twitter?

A skit from Sade's 2010 appearance on The Wanda Sykes Show resurfaced online during February of 2022, causing fans to once again give praise.

In the clip, Sade tells Wanda she's on her way to film Jimmy Kimmel Live!.

Wanda informs the musician she should do her show instead, saying it's Black History Month.

The user who uploaded the video captioned their tweet: "that time when sade interacted with us then disappeared for over a decade."

Sade has not released an album since 2010.

Who is Sade Adu?

Born on January 16, 1959, Sade Adu is a singer, songwriter, and actress.

She's known as one of the most successful British female artists of all-time, and has won four Grammys throughout her career thus far.

In 2002, Sade was appointed as an Officer of the Order of the British Empire for her services to music.

She was later promoted to Commander of the British Empire in 2017.

Sade has six studio albums, including: Diamond Life, Promise, Stronger Than Pride, Love Deluxe, Lovers Rock, and Soldier of Love.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Sade has an estimated net worth of around $70million.

Who is Wanda Sykes?

Born on March 7, 1964, Wanda Sykes is a popular comedian, actress, and writer.

During the 1990s, Wanda rose to prominence for her work on The Chris Rock Show - a position that earned the star her first Primetime Emmy Award in 1999.

She was named one of the top 25 Funniest People in America during a 2004 story from Entertainment Weekly.

Throughout her career, Wanda has appeared in a multitude of popular series, including Curb Your Enthusiasm, Black-ish, and The Upshaws.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Wanda Sykes has an estimated net worth of around $10million.
