The TV schedule has come under fire across all channels this Christmas with many irritated by the number of repeat shows.

However, in among the rehashed and compilation shows there has been plenty of new material that attracted viewers in their millions.

The pick of the bunch was the return of Gavin and Stacey with it winning the Christmas Day TV ratings war with 12.31million tuning in.

It was the largest audience recorded that day in a decade with half (49.2 per cent) of all TV viewers tuned in and an average of 11.5million viewers.

The Queen's Christmas Broadcast was the next most-watched programme, with Strictly Come Dancing, EastEnders and Michael McIntyre's Big Show finishing in the top five.
