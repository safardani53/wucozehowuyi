Forever 21 adjusts Christmas hours at all 410 stores to give staff time off but won't completely clo

Publish date: 2024-02-17

CUSTOMERS needing a last minute holiday outfit should plan ahead this year if they want to shop at this one store.

Forever 21 is following suit behind other retailers like Target and Walmart.

The popular fast fashion retailer will be closed on Christmas Day.

This is routine for Forever 21 which is also typically closed on Easter Sunday and Thanksgiving Day.

The retailer also doesn't differ from most large chains with nationwide closings on December 25 — but the holiday observance will mean that customers must take advantage of the store's holiday dresses and blinged out accessories before time runs out.

The store generally operates from 10 am to 9 pm on Monday-Saturday and from 12 pm to 6 pm on Sundays, though these times are subject to change based on location.

Read more on holiday hours

A Forever 21 employee told The U.S. Sun that the store is business as usual on Christmas Eve though, operating on regular store hours, from 11 am to 8 pm.

Some Forever 21 stores are regularly open from 10 am to 8 pm across the country.

We recommend that you call ahead to check the exact Forever 21 holiday hours of operation that you'd like to shop at.

While Forever 21 does not list its holiday hours of operation on its website, holiday hours for its contact line are available.

For customers with questions wanting to call the store, Forever 21's hotline is open from 5 am to 5 pm (PST) on Christmas Eve as opposed to its usual 5 am to 9 pm (PST).

All lines are closed on Christmas Day.

The same times apply for New Year's Eve.

MORE CHRISTMAS CLOSURES

Many stores want their employees home for the holidays.

Target will also be closing all of its approximately 2,000 nationwide stores on Christmas day.

Much like Walmart, many Target stores will also be closing early on Christmas Eve at 8 pm instead of the usual 10 pm.

Walmart will also be closing its approximately 4,500 nationwide stores on December 25.

Shoppers will have their final chance to score last-minute gifts on Christmas Eve, when most stores will be open from 6 am until 6 pm

For book lovers, Christmas Day is also not the time to shop.

Iconic book retailer Barnes & Noble will also be closing its approximately 600 stores on Christmas Day.
