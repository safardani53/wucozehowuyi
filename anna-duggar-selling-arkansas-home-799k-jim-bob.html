ANNA Duggar is selling an Arkansas home for $799,000 after she bought the abode from her father-in-law Jim Bob for only $257,000. 

In Arkansas property records exclusively obtained by The Sun, Josh's wife Anna, 32, is selling a five-bedroom, four-bathroom home in Springdale, Arkansas for $799,000.

A description for the home read: “An eagle's perch view in this unique opportunity includes one of the highest points in Benton County offering spectacular views on sitting of 5.22 beautiful acres.”

The lodge-style home features stone walls and floors. 

The home is in need of work, as the bids to complete the residence are in the $450,000 to $550,000 range. 

An insider exclusively told The Sun: “The home has no finished work, it’s a shell. 

Anna listed the family property as:

“Jim Bob and Michelle bought a different house up there and flipped it for $1 million or something. This house was built by the same people. 

“Somebody else who bought it from them is trying to sell it for way too high.”

Jim Bob, 55, first bought the property in October 2016 for $325,000, according to property records.

He then sold it to Soli Deo Gloria, an LLC in Anna’s name, for $257,078 in June 2019.
