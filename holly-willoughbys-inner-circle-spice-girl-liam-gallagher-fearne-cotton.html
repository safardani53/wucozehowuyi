Inside Holly Willoughbys VERY glam inner circle, including a Spice Girl and Liam Gallaghers ex

Publish date: 2024-06-24

HOLLY Willoughby is the type of friend every woman wants to have, with her down-to-earth personality, oh-so amazing fashion sense and love of a few drinks.

But these are the women lucky enough to be included in the mum-of-three's very glam inner circle, including a Spice Girl and Liam Gallagher's ex.

Meet the Willo-babes...

Holly, 37, surrounds herself with four equally gorgeous gals, who are her go-to mates for girls' holidays and boozy nights out in Soho.

Whether she needs tips on how to cope with her three kids, advice of what shoes she can pull off or is just in need of a large glass of wine after a long day at work - this quartet are the people she turns to.

So who are they?

Nicole Appleton - The Party Animal

We all know Holly loves a good ol' knees but the lady who's often holding her hand at the bar is Nicole Appleton, 43, is the gal who KNOWS how to party.

Every time Holly hits the town with the All Saints singer she knows she's going to have a good one.

"While she is good fun, Nicole is quite wary and doesn’t trust people easily but her inner circle like Holly are her rocks," an insider who knows the gang told Fabulous.

"When she’s not with the other girls touring or recording, Holly et al are the ones she turns to for fun and advice. She and Emma have a special bond as they lived through the crazy 90s together when it was all huge. Old rivalry’s don’t mean anything now they’re all in their 40s."

Like Holly, Nicole is also a mum to Gene (who she has with her ex-husband Liam Gallagher's) and she can be known for liking an innocent flirt every once in a while too. And why the devil not!

Life for Nicole is about laughing, having fun and not giving a s**t about what others thing and Holly LOVE her for just that!

Emma Bunton - The Mummy Mate

Once Baby Spice, now baby-mamma - former Spice girl and mum-of-two, Emma is the women who knows what's best to use when your little one has a nasty nappy-rash and, like Holly, knows how to face the day when your night has been broken up with the lil' nippers having nightmares.

As well as hosting the breakfast show on Heart FM, Emma is the co-founder Kit & Kin, which is an eco-friendly nappy and mum and baby product collection.

Emma, 42, is no doubt the lady on Holly's What's App at the crack of dawn, as let's be honest, the world is still asleep when these two are off to work!

Shiarra Bell - The Thinker

Whether you're after a night of drinking red wine and debating politics, issues or just life in general then Shiarra is there.

Shiarra, is head of artist relations at Universal Music, a mum-of-two and wife of Ride rocker Andy Bell.

Originally from Ireland, but living in London, she the girl in the gang who knows her mind, is determined but is also endlessly loyal to her gal pals.

"Shiarra is from the old school music industry and met Nicole and Emma Bunton back in the 90s when she was a junior and they were hitting the big time," an insider told us.

"She parties hard with the rest of them but is a shoulder to cry on has the best advice.

"She’s the one who ‘gets things done’ as she’s not a known face and is used to helping out the celebs in her daily work, she can secure them the coolest and most discreet places to go."

Like her character, Shiarra's Instagram is full of messages about positive thinking, karma and being Irish to her core.

Niki de Metz: The Shopper 

Holly and her girls ALWAYS look good but when it comes to knowing those up and coming brands, who's wearing what and what you should be wearing and when it's PR guru and socialite, Niki.

When she's not looking effortlessly fabulous, like all the ladies in the Holly-Babes, Niki loves a night with the girls drinking cocktails and wine until dawn.

She is married to fellow PR guru Charlie Lycett.

Where do they hang out?

These yummy mummies love a boozy night out on the town, with Aperol Spritz, Espresso martinis, Champagne and wine as the tipples of choice.

Holly may cut a slim figure, but she and the girls still love a good Dirty Burger.

Their fave joints are sushi restaurant Nobu in Park Lane, Mayfair oyster bar Scott's, and exclusive members clubs Soho House and The Arts Club.

Emma, Holly, Nicole, Shiarra and Niki are also regulars at Oxfordshire's star-studded Soho Farmhouse - and the girl gang have also been snapped at a flurry of gigs together.

Who else is in the gang?

While Nicole and Emma are two of Holly's closest mates, who even kiss on the lips as a sign of affection, having met through the celeb circuit.

The This Morning presenter became close to Shiarra and Niki during a girls' trip to the party island of Ibiza back in 2013 - as the girls rallied round Nicole, who was going through her divorce from her cheating ex Liam at the time.

But through their work and connections - the girls' gang also includes a whole host of other famous faces...

Tamzin Outhwaite, 47: EastEnders star, actress and mum-of-two also hangs out with the gals as she happens to be the cousin of Holly's TV producer husband Dan Baldwin, 43.

Myleene Klass, 40: Hear'Say singer, model and mum-of-three.

Nat Appleton, 45: All Saints singer, Nicole's lookalike sister and mum-of-two.

Kate Thornton, 45: TV presenter, Loose Women panellist and mum-of-one.

Where's Fearne Cotton gone?

  • Fearne Cotton, 37, and Holly used to be inseparable - but a lot seems to have changed over the last 10 years.
  • The TV presenter was a bridesmaid at Holly's 2007 wedding, while the pair have starred in Celebrity Juice together since 2008.
  • They even released a book together, called The Best Friend's Guide To Life, in 2010.
  • But these days their friendship doesn't seem to go beyond the walls of the ITV studios.
  • Fearne is never pictured with Holly's girl gang.
  • While the mum-of-two seems to spend more time with the likes of Davina McCall, Emma Willis, Gok Wan and her husband Jesse Wood (son of rocker Ronnie) than her former BFF.
  • Appearing on the cover of Women's Health this month, Fearne admitted her social life has gone "down the s***ter" since the birth of her kids Rex, five, and Honey, three.
  • She said she struggled to juggle work and motherhood, so had to sacrifice her friendships, but added: "My girlfriends aren't going anywhere."
