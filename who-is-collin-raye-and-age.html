Who is Collin Raye and how old is he?

Collin Raye, 61, born Floyd Elliot Wray from De Queen, Arkansas, is a country music singer known for his album, All I Can Be.

Raye was once married to a woman named Connie in 1980, however, they later got divorced.

The pair have a daughter, Brittany, and a son, Jacob.

As the first of four albums by Raye, All I Can Be achieved platinum certification in the U.S. for one million sold copies during its time.

Raye has charted 30 singles on the U.S. country charts between 1991 and 2007.

What happened his brother, Scotty Wray?

On Friday, February 18, 2022, Raye's brother, Scotty Wray, passed away. He was allegedly battling heart issues over the last few years.

He was a familiar face to country music fans and well known as the longtime guitarist for Miranda Lambert, 38.

The late musician had been on the road, by Lambert's side for over 20 years, until he was forced to retire from touring in order to focus on his pressing health issues.

Lambert took to social media to share a heartfelt tribute:

"Scotty Wray was family to me and I’ll never sing a note without him because I know he is there with me. He always has been. I love you my sweet Bud Wray. Heaven is lucky cause that honky tonk band up there just gained another guitar pickin angel. Rest easy my love," she wrote on Instagram with a picture of the two showing off matching arrow tattoos.
