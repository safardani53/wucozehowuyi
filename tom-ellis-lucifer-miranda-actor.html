Who is Tom Ellis? Lucifer and Miranda star

Publish date: 2024-06-22

TOM Ellis went from British TV star to international megastar when he landed the role of Lucifer.

He is now back with a brand with season 5B of the show, but fans are keen to know what he gets up to behind closed doors.

Who is Tom Ellis?

42-year-old Welsh actor Tom Ellis has starred in Lucifer since 2015.

Before that, Tom was best known for starring in the BBC sitcom Miranda.

He was also well known for playing Dr Oliver Cousins in EastEnders.

In the Netflix series Lucifer, he plays the leading role of Lucifer Morningstar.

Who is Tom Ellis married to?

In June 2019, Tom married his long-term girlfriend, American screenwriter and actress Meaghan Oppenheimer.

The pair tied the knot in a lavish ceremony at the Grassini Family Winery in Santa Ynez Valley, California.

They began dating in 2015 and announced their engagement in 2018.

When did Tom Ellis split from Tamzin Outhwaite?

Tom wed EastEnders star Tamzin Outhwaite in 2006 in Somerset after meeting the year before through their mutual friend James McAvoy.

They went on to have have two daughters, Florence (born 2008) and Marnie (born 2012).

But in 2014, following revelations of Tom's infidelity, the couple divorced.

Tamzin was left devastated after he confessed to a one-night stand while filming Gothica in the States in 2013.

In an interview in 2016, Tamzin hinted it was more than "a one night stand" as he had claimed.

She said: “I wouldn't not be with my husband over a one-night stand is all I'll say.'

What is Tom Ellis' net worth?

According to The Richest, Tom has a net worth of $6M (£4.5M) .

This is largely down to his work on TV.

It's estimated his Netflix earnings for Lucifer could be as high as $50K (£37K) per episode.
