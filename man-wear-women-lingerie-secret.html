DEAR DEIDRE: I AM addicted to wearing women’s lingerie even though I know I should only wear male clothes.

I am a 41-year-old single man. I find knickers far more comfy than men’s pants.

I have also thought about wearing dresses, skirts and shoes and want to try make-up and perfume too.

I’m starting to go out wearing women’s lingerie under my clothes, even to work. I love how they feel next to my skin.

I would love to get a girlfriend who would accept this side of me, as I don’t seem to be able to change.

I do wonder, though, if this is an odd thing to do.
