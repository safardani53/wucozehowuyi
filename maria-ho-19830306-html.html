Maria Ho is a Taiwanese-American poker player and television host/commentator. One of the top ranked female poker players in the world, and a Women in Poker Hall of Fame inductee, she has over $4,000,000 in live tournament winnings. Ho's live tournament poker record includes 63 WSOP cashes, 5 World Series of Poker final tables, 13 World Poker Tour cashes, 4 WPT final tables, and numerous additional final tables on the professional poker circuit. Outside of poker, she has been seen on many primetime television shows; making it to "Hollywood Week" on season 3 of American Idol, competing as the only all-female team on the fifteenth season of The Amazing Race and appearing as a celebrity guest on Deal or No Deal in January 2019.
