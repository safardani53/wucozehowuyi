TEEN Mom star Kiaya Elliot's baby daddy, X'zayveon Gambrell, was released from prison after four years behind bars. 

A Department of Corrections official exclusively revealed to The Sun that X'zayveon, 21, was released from the Pocahontas State Correctional Center on May 9, 2022, and is currently out on parole and under supervision in Virginia. 

Kiaya, 21, gave birth to their son Amour, 4, after X'zayveon was already in custody on charges of receiving a stolen firearm, felony possession of weapon/ammo (not a gun), and possession of marijuana with intent to distribute. 

According to RadarOnline, he was sentenced to five years behind bars and was denied parole in 2019 because of his "extensive criminal record, and serious nature and circumstances of offenses."

The parole board added that they had concluded he "should serve more of your sentence prior to release on parole" and "the board concludes you to be a risk to the community." 

His release date was set for January 9, 2023, but he was released early.

Another woman filed an assault charge against the MTV star and accused her of pointing/brandishing a gun at him.  

Kiaya was arrested on March 25, 2021, and later bailed out for $3,500. 

She joked about the arrest on a TikTok, filming herself laughing and covering her face.
