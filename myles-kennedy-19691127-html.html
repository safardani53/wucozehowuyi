Myles Richard Bass, professionally known as Myles Kennedy, is an American musician, singer, and songwriter best known as the band's lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist. Rock Alter Bridge, and lead vocalist in guitarist Slash's support group, known as Myles Kennedy and the Conspirators. A former guitar instructor from Spokane, Washington, he worked as a musician and version musician, appeared both in the studio and live with a number of artists, and was involved in a number of projects throughout his career. Born in Boston, Massachusetts, and raised in northern Idaho and Spokane, Kennedy attended Spokane Falls Community College to study music theory. He began his musical career in 1990 as the lead guitarist of the concert jazz band Cosmic Dust, with whom he released a studio album. His second group, Citizen Swing, released two studio albums before disbanding in 1996
