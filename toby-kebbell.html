Toby Kebbell Biography

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Toby Kebbell was born on in South Elmsall, to Michelle, a landscape gardener and cook. His father Robert Kebbell, on the other hand, is an engineer.

He was raised in Nottingham by his mother, as a single parent, after the divorce with his dad. He attended Catholic primary school.

Growing up, Kebbell got interested in the entertainment industry. Thus, he started training in acting at the Central Junior Television Workshop in Nottingham. He got celebrity recognition when director Shane Meadows cast him in the film “Dead Man’s Shoe” as Anthony. For the role, he was nominated for Most Promising Newcomer at the “British Independent Film Awards.

Subsequently, he did a role of Mark Sherbert and Pausanias of Orestis in the movie “Northern Soul” and “Alexander” respectively.

After playing minor roles in “Match Point” and “Wilderness”, Kebbell once again ruled the film industry portraying the character Rob Gretton in “Control”. For his role, he was awarded “British Independent Film Award” for Best Supporting Actor as well as was nominated for “ALFA Award” for British Supporting Actor of the year.

Adding up, he has done numerous hit movies including, “War for the Planet”, “Gold”, “Warcraft”, Buddha’s Little Finger”, “Fantastic Four”, “The East”, and “Ben-Hur”.

Apart from the movie, Kebbell has also starred in various TV series such as “Peak Practice”, “ShakespeaRe-Told”, “Born Equal”, “The Street”, “Black Mirror” and “The Escape Artist’.

Kebbell, 35, is neither married nor single. Currently, he is in an open relationship with his long-time girlfriend, Danielle. The lovebird often gives public appearances together. It seems the duo is pretty serious in their relationship and might take their relationship to whole new level someday.

Going back to his previous days, Kebbell had an affair with Ruzwana Bashir, a British director. They met for the first time at a Christmas party by Andrew Neil.

The Hollywood star Kebbell has an estimated net worth of around $6 million. The significant source of his earning is his professional acting career.
