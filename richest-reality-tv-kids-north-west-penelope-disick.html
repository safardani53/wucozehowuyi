Meet the richest reality TV kids featuring North West, 9, Penelope Disick, 10, and Ryder Wharton, 6

Publish date: 2024-02-03

THEIR parents may give up their privacy, but reality TV stars are well compensated.

The high paydays of some of the most famous reality celebs often trickle down to their children - who enjoy a wealth all of their own.

Take Kim Kardashian's daughter, North, for instance.

The pre-teen has a growing collection of expensive handbags including a $23,000 Chanel tote bag and a $35,000 Prada purse.

 North may only be nine years old but she lives a life that's just as rich in luxury as her mom, her famous aunts, and other members of the famous family.

She kicks it with big-name celebrities like Ice Spice, is outfitted in designer clothes and shoes, and generally doesn't seem to want for much.

NORTH, SAINT, CHICAGO AND PSALM WEST

Perhaps no kids are more spoiled than those of the Kardashians

Kim, 42, and her ex husband Kanye West share North, nine, Saint, seven, Chicago, five, and Psalm, three.

All are said to be worth tens of millions of dollars.

The Kardashians often spare no expense when keeping their children active and entertained - such as the over-the-top playgrounds and playhouses they've constructed for their children.

The backyard play areas include several examples, such as Kim's $70,000 personalized miniature town and Kylie's childhood home replica playhouse.

The Skims founder had an elaborate miniature village built for her four children in the backyard of her $60million Calabasas estate.

Kim created a child-friendly town featuring a KKW Beauty boutique, Lego castle, and fire station which she christened "Lil Hidden Hills."

Visitors can enjoy a blue 50s-style diner named "West Diner," a grocery store, a flower shop, a miniaturized Starbucks and two-story cottages.

PENELOPE DISICK

Penelope, 10, is the daughter of Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick.

She gained fame at a young age when she made frequent appearances on the popular television show Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

Earlier this month, the preteen showed off some snaps of her spoils, when she flaunted her designer closet, featuring a $1.3K Gucci coat.

The photos came from a trip the young girl took with her mom to Paris in 2020.

In one pic Penelope posed for the camera in some leopard print pants, a black t-shirt and an incredible Gucci coat.

Kourtney also gave a sneak peek at what other amazing items of clothing she rocked on the trip, including another jacket from the popular designer.

RYDER WHARTON AND ACE DAVIS

Cheyenne Floyd's two kids flash their mom's wealth in fancy clothing.

The Teen Mom OG alum shares Ryder, 6, with her ex-boyfriend Cory Wharton, 31, and Ace, 1, with her husband Zach Davis, 32.

The 30-year-old mom has been slammed in the past for dressing Ace in a pair of $160 Burberry shorts & $200 top, as he tottled around his house.

Before that, she dressed daughter, Ryder in a $400 Versace designer swimsuit during a pricey family vacation to Hawaii.

The white one-piece had blue, pink, and purple butterflies all over it, with the designer logo across the front.

In other photos, Ryder sported a nearly $200 swimsuit from luxury brand MC2 Saint Barth.

STORMI WEBSTER

Each year, five-year-old Stormi's parents,  Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott throw the pre-schooler a blowout birthday party.

Kylie threw one of the maddest celebrity parties ever for her daughter Stormi's first birthday - dubbed Stormi World.

Inspired by her dad's third album, Astroworld, guests were greeted at the party by a massive inflatable head which looked like Stormi.

And inside, the soiree only got more surreal with three-story slides, a carousel, a swing ride and Stormi-themed party games.

Since then, subsequent editions of Stormi World have been held, each more extravagant than the last.

TRUE THOMPSON

Khloe Kardashian's daughter, True Thompson, spent this just-passed Easter Sunday in a $1.2k pink Gucci dress.

True was all smiles in the photos, as she sported an all-pink ensemble including a bow headband on her head as well as the pricey Gucci dress.

As for jewelry, True showed off a necklace with her name on it as well as a gold cross. She also wore two bracelets on her arms.

Days earlier, Khloe threw True an Octonauts-themed fifth birthday bash at her $17 million Los Angeles mansion.

The party included a two-tier birthday cake, decorated with blue icing and covered with detailed characters from the kids' series.

There was also a slime-making station, personalized backpacks, extravagant decorations, and a piñata filled with candy.

SOPHIA ABRAHAM

Farrah Abraham's daughter, Sophia, has made her money by charging fans as much as $25,000 in exchange for her follow on any social media platform.

The 14-year-old, who was born to Farrah when she was just a teenager, has claimed she will "follow you on the social platform of your choosing" for the insane amount.

Fans were more than displeased, as one took to Facebook to write: "This must be bulls**t."

Some even believed Farrah was behind the wild offering, one commenting: "Is her mother exposing her as well to all these creeps?

ALABAMA BARKER

Alabama and her rock star dad, Travis Barker, entered a new tax bracket when he married Kourtney Kardashian.

Since then the 17-year-old has continuously flaunted her family fortune.

Among them, a Balenciaga hourglass mini handbag, including a black shiny crocodile embossed calfskin.

Her rocker dad pulled out all the stops for the present, as the bag has a price tag of $1,300, according to the brand's website.

Alabama credited her father in her caption: "I love it! thank you @travisbarker."

DANNIELYNN BIRKHEAD

Dannielynn Birkhead has been without her mother, Anna Nicole Smith, since she was five months old, but she's never been without her money.

Anna Nicole passed away at 39 in 2007 after an accidental overdose.

Dannielynn is now a 16-year-old who spends most of her time avoiding the spotlight.

But there is one day of the year she and her father, Larry Birkhead, come into the spotlight - The Kentucky Derby.

The duo attend the Barnstable Brown Kentucky Derby Gala in Louisville, Kentucky every year.

That party also happens to be where Larry met Anna Nicole for the first time.

The pair dresses up for the occasion, and often honor the memory of Anna Nicole.

JOJO SIWA

JoJo Siwa made a name for herself as a multimillion-dollar singer and dancer whose stardom was elevated by her Nickelodeon show.

The 19-year-old gained popularity from her daily videos of her day-to-day life on her YouTube channel, "Its JoJo Siwa."

According to CelebrityNetWorth, JoJo has an impressive net worth of $20million.

Since leaving Dance Moms in its sixth season, JoJo has found a strong following on YouTube, where she's amassed over 12 million subscribers.

Her videos include challenges, confessionals and behind-the-scenes clips of her celebrity lifestyle.
