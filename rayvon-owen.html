Rayvon Owen Profile Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Bio:

Rayvon Owen (born on June 27, 1991 in Richmond, Virginia) is an American singer. He graduated from Belmont University in 2013. He auditioned for the 14th season of American Idol in San Francisco, CA with the song “Wide Awake” by Katy Perry.

Best Known For:

Rayvon is best known for competing on the 14th season of American Idol.

Personal Life:

Prior to his time on American Idol, Rayon had been working as a vocal coach in his hometown of Richmond.
