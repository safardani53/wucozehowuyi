Should I wash my face after using makeup remover wipes?

Absolutely!

This wipe is used for removing makeup, not your every day/night cleanser.

Don't forget to use your cleanser after wiping your face with makeup removers.

Are makeup remover wipes bad for your skin?

Unfortunately, makeup wipes are inherently harsh on your skin.

They don't clean as thoroughly as a dedicated facial cleanser and water, often leaving behind grime and oil which can over time lead to clogged pores and breakouts.

They'll help remove part of your makeup or surface bacteria but they're unable to effectively breakdown the makeup, oil and dirt.
