And Just Like That cranks up the sex with outrageous romps including full nudity and threesomes seco

Publish date: 2024-07-18

FANS were left disappointed when the first series of Sarah Jessica Parker’s And Just Like That proved to have far less sex in the city than its predecessor. 

So they will be delighted to hear that series two is packed with raunchy scenes, beginning just seconds into the first episode with a two-minute-long montage of bedroom action.

The Sex And The City originals - Carrie Bradshaw, Charlotte York and Miranda Hobbes - all get down and dirty, as well as their new pals Seema Patel and Lisa Todd Wexley.

Miranda’s fully nude romp, with lesbian squeeze Che Diaz, even takes place in a swimming pool. 

And the rompfest doesn’t stop there, with the seven-part series packed with eye popping moments akin to the HBO original which was known for pushing the boundaries on women unashamedly owning their sexuality. 

Later, there is a graphic scene where Miranda (Cynthia Nixon) tries on a leather harness and strap-on dildo as she explores her new sex life with a woman.

She even indulges in a threesome.

And Seema (Sarita Choudhury) is later also grappling with gear after bedding a man who stops mid-session to use a penis pump. 

In another scene, Seema, Carrie (Sarah Jessica Parker) and Nya Wallace (Karen Pittman) go looking for no-strings sex with strangers at a hotel bar. 

Elsewhere, Charlotte and Lisa are scandalised - but delighted - by their inclusion on a 'MILF list' created by a teenager at their children's school.

It’s the kind of storyline fans loved in the then-groundbreaking Sex And The City and missed from the debut season of And Just Like That. 

This series also marks the shock return of Kim Cattrall as beloved character Samantha Jones.

She filmed her scenes separately from the other women after years of tension.

But SJP said this week: “It was just a nice nod to the 25 years [of Sex And The City]."

Kristin Davis, who plays Charlotte, agreed: “The fans miss Samantha. The fans love Samantha. Samantha’s a fantastic character. We get it. 

“It seemed like something that would be great to do.”

And Just Like That… is exclusively available tomorrow (June 22) on Sky Comedy and streaming service NOW.
