LINDSAY Lohan has resurfaced for a Pilates class selfie to show off her major weight loss only five months after giving birth.

The Mean Girls star uploaded the workout selfie to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday.

Simply captioned "Pilates," Lindsay, 37, photographed herself standing alone inside the Pilates studio.

The Canyons actress showed off her super slim, post-baby silhouette as posed beside some exercise equipment.

Lindsay sported a pair of form-fitting white leggings and a black tank top for the workout session.

She also had her famous red hair pulled back into a fitness-focused bun as she snapped the photo.
