Gwen Stefani looks unrecognizable in 'spooky' corpse bride Halloween costume and boasts she did elab

Publish date: 2024-03-19

GWEN Stefani has shown off her spooky Halloween costume that included a full-face makeup look she did herself.

The Voice coach uploaded a new video to Instagram on Tuesday to give fans a look at her elaborate costume.

Gwen, 54, took on the holiday as a spooky corpse bride, appearing to mimic the character Emily Merrimack.

Her look was composed of a lace, white wedding dress, completed with a matching lace veil and finger-wrapped gloves.

The Rich Girl singer added a flower crown made of muted tones with butterfly accents.

For her hair and makeup, Gwen opted for a blue wig and painted her entire face a shade of gradient blues.

She had a darker blue eyeshadow on and used the same shade to contour her nose and alter her face shape for the spooky look.

She had long, dramatic eyelashes on to mimic the spider that was seen on her chest.

However, the No Doubt frontwoman still opted to rock her signature red lip color, which has become a staple in her GXVE makeup collection.

"So spooooky !! gx [spider emoji] [ghost emoji] makeup by me:)," Gwen declared in her caption.

RED LIP SEASON

Gwen also shared another video to her Instagram on Tuesday as she promoted a separate career away from her current gig on The Voice.

The video was shared alongside Marianna Hewitt, who co-founded the makeup and skincare line Summer Fridays.

Both Gwen and Marianna were seen applying red lipstick from her GXVE makeup line, followed by a similar-colored butter balm from Summer Fridays.

The Cool singer appeared to be wearing a white bra top, which allowed her to show off a bit of cleavage as she went in circles with lipstick around her lips.

The wife of Blake Shelton provided a link in the IG story, which read: "Just 2 girlies that love beauty !!"

The link went to the original TikTok video, shared by Marianna, officially claiming it to be "red lip season."

Just one day before, Gwen gave her followers another exciting career update.
