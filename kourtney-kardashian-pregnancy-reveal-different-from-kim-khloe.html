Kourtney Kardashian praised for 'sweet' pregnancy announcement as sisters Kim &amp; Khloe revealed baby

Publish date: 2024-04-20

KOURTNEY Kardashian fans have praised the star for the incredible way she announced her pregnancy.

Throughout their lifetimes, the Kardashian family has announced many pregnancies — and most have been through their former reality show: Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

More recently, other baby-related news was announced and revealed on Hulu's The Kardashians.

However, unlike her sisters Kim, 42, and Khloe, 38, Kourtney, 44, announced her pregnancy in a different way.

Kim and Khloe have revealed their pregnancies through various episodes during their show's 20-season run.

Kim has four children with rapper Kanye West, 46: North, 10; Saint, seven; Chicago, five; and Psalm, four.

Meanwhile, Khloe has two children with basketball star Tristan Thompson, 32: True, five, and a one-year-old son, Tatum.

Kourtney has three kids with ex-Scott Disick, 40: Mason, 13; Penelope, 10; and Reign, eight.

Kourtney announced that she was pregnant with her fourth child — and first with her husband, Travis Barker, 47 on Friday night at the rocker's Los Angeles gig with Blink-182, holding up a huge sign reading: "Travis I'm pregnant."

The surprise declaration was a nod to Blink's iconic All The Small Things video in which an attractive groupie holds up a similar sign while rocking out to the band.

'REALLY CUTE!'

On a popular Kardashian internet forum, many fans praised the way Kourtney shared the news.

The original poster explained the "Travis I'm Pregnant" reference and fans thought it was "special."

"It’s really cute," one Reddit user said.

A second wrote: "This makes her announcement that much sweeter, I’m so happy for them."

"So cute. At first I was like 'hmm that’s an interesting way to announce it.' This makes it so much more special that she really planned out how she was going to tell him," another pointed out.

"Life imitates art: " a fourth stated.

A fifth admitted: "Such an iconic way to announce it!"

CONGRATS KOURT AND TRAV!

The good news brought the concert to a halt as stunned Travis left his drum kit to go and celebrate with his wife, who was standing in front of the stage.

The pair shared a sweet cuddle and a kiss as the crowd around them went wild.

Frontman Mark Hoppus whipped fans into a frenzy, making sure everyone in the stadium knew what was going on as Kourtney and Travis had their special moment.

PREGNANCY CLUES

Just days ago, fans speculated the couple were expecting after Kourtney posted shots of a carb-heavy feast amid pregnancy rumors.

The Hulu star took to her Instagram Stories and shared two pics of her fancy dinner out, as the food was a great departure from her usual keto diet.

For the post, Kourtney snapped a couple of photos of her meal at Tre Lune; a restaurant she previously visited with Travis for her 44th birthday in April.

First up, the reality TV personality snapped a shot of a light green soup in front of her.

The Lemme founder also had a bowl of freshly-cut bread, along with a bright green dip.

Kourtney also added a long tray of homemade breadsticks to her order.

In the next pic, Kourtney captured her main course - golden risotto with fresh asparagus - while the rest of the dishes seemed to have been eaten.

The bread basket had been nibbled at, while the breadsticks had almost been entirely eaten, with the bread dip gone completely.

Alongside all of the dishes, the only visible drink on the table was a glass of cold water.

FAMILY DREAMS

During Season 2 of The Kardashians, Kourtney opened up about her attempts to get pregnant - including IVF.

“Travis and I want to have a baby, and so my doctor took us down this road of doing IVF,” Kourtney said in a confessional during one episode.

“Like, it hasn’t been the most amazing experience," she added.
