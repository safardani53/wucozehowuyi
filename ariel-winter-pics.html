Pics Of The Modern Family Star Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Ariel Winter, 21, first came to fame when ‘Modern Family’ premiered in September 2009. For the past 10 years, Ariel played Alex Dunphy, the smart, nerdy, and cute middle child of Claire and Phil, the polar opposite of her popular, fun-loving big sister Haley. Her star power has risen over the past decade as she became one of the most sought-after actors in young Hollywood. Ariel has also made headlines for her personal life. The actress unfortunately had to deal with a toxic relationship with her mother, who has bashed her publicly. Her beloved older sister had custody of her as a teenager so that she could escape her emotional turmoil. She’s doing better and thriving as an adult! She was also scrutinized for her decision to get breast reduction surgery, despite the fact that she was equally scrutinized for looking busty in her red carpet outfits. She always looks fabulous, though. Ariel is currently in a relationship with Levi Meaden.
