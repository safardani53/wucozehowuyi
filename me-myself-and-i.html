Reylynn Caster - Me, Myself &amp; I Cast Member

Publish date: 2024-02-28

Reylynn Caster was born and raised in Wichita, Kan. until she was 13 years old. She began taking acting classes and became involved with local theatre where she appeared on stage in numerous productions, including “Big River,” “The Music Man,” “Aladdin,” “Pippin,” “Sleeping Beauty” and “Willy Wonka.”

After her audition tape for the film “Logan” caught the eye of talent agents, she signed with an agency and moved to Los Angeles. Her first major acting role was a guest spot in the television series “Speechless.”

Caster loves helping animals and is interested in charitable organizations, such as “no kill” animal shelters. She saved all of her allowance for one year and bought her first dog, Rosie.

Her birthday is March 3. A fun fact about her birthday: she was born on March 3, 2003 at 11:11. Follow her on Twitter @ReylynnCaster and Instagram @ReylynnC.
