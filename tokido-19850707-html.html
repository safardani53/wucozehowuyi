Hajime Taniguchi, also known as Tokido, is a Japanese fighting game player who plays the role of franchising the King of Fighters and Street Fighter franchises. He is known to play several fighting games on a competitive level apart from Street Fighter, including Tekken, Marvel vs Capcom 3 and BlazBlue. Tokido is a three-time EVO champion, winning Capcom against SNK 2 in 2002 as well as Super Street Fighter II Turbo in 2007 and Street Fighter V ten years later. Tokido has had over 19 Top 8 Evolution rankings in 9 different games in 15 years, an achievement that only surpassed Justin Wong.
