RHOBHs Kyle Richards says family was devastated by niece Paris Hiltons sex tape scandal

Publish date: 2024-10-04

RHOBH's Kyle Richards revealed her family was 'devastated' when her niece's Paris Hilton's sex tape leaked.

The famous scandal came up when the RHOBH star appeared on Watch What Happens Live.

Appearing on last night Watch What Happens Live host Andy Cohen asked Kyle whether she thought Paris' 2004 sex tape leak would catapult or ruin her career.

Kyle answered: “We were devastated.

“It was horrible.”

Paris agreed, saying of the "1 Night in Paris" tape with her ex-boyfriend Rick Salomon in a 2014 interview: “It was devastating because that [Salomon] was someone I was with for a few years,'

“I don’t think I’ll ever be able to fully trust any man again after that. It was just the most hurtful and awful thing that anyone could do to a little girl.

"I was very young, it wasn’t my fault.”

Paris later said in the 2018 documentary The American Meme she "felt like everyone on the street was laughing at me,” after the tape leaked.

The 39-year-old who is now dating Carter Reum went on of the experience: “It was like being raped.”

She said: “I was like, ‘I just don’t want to live’ because I thought everything was taken away from me.

“Like, I didn’t want to be known as that. I would never be who I could have been.”
