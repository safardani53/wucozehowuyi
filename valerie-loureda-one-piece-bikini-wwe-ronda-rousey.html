Valerie Loureda stuns in revealing one-piece bikini as new WWE star calls out Ronda Rousey for dream

Publish date: 2024-07-31

WWE signing Valerie Loureda is looking right at home in her new surroundings - and eyeing a dream fight against Ronda Rousey.

The former Bellator star, 23, has joined Rousey in making the crossover from MMA to the world of wrestling.

Fighting out of Miami, Loureda made her name in the Octagon and has a pro record of 4-1.

She last fought in November 2021 and made a brief cameo at WrestleMania 38 earlier this year

Loureda is the first woman of Cuban-American descent to sign with WWE and declared she is "here to take over".

Valeria was ringside on a dramatic night at WWE Money in the Bank as she celebrated her new role.

And Loureda wants to cut her teeth in wrestling with the very best, calling out Ronda ahead of her eagerly awaited debut.

She told TMZ: "Definitely, Ronda [I'd like to face].
