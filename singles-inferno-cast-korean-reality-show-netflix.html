Single's Inferno cast: Who is taking part in the Korean reality show?

Publish date: 2024-09-03

THE Single's Inferno Season Three cast have stunned on screen - here's everything you need to know.

Fans were thrilled to meet every contestant as the third episode aired last night on Netflix.

Who is in the cast of Single's Inferno season 3?

Kim Gyu-ri

Kim Gyu-ri is best known for entering as a contestant on Miss Korea 2022.

The 28-year-old cat-lover has amassed an impressive 25.6k followers on her Instagram account.

She has made her way up in the modelling industry, featuring in campaigns for Korean brands.

Choi Hye-seon

Choi Hye-seon has been dubbed a "ball of sunshine" and described herself as a "bubbly, outgoing" person.

The 24-year-old is studying natural sciences at the well-respected  Ehwa Women’s University.

Her Instagram platform has 60.8k followers and she often showcases gorgeous snaps from her modelling shoots.

Choi Min-woo

Choi Min-Woo has expressed his intentions of finding a classy, mature woman on the hot dating show.

He described himself as "shy and introverted" and has not yet revealed how old he is.

Previous career highlights include modelling stints and appearing for Esquire Hong Kong.

Lee Jin-seok

Businessman Lee Jin-Seok owns multiple cafes in Daegu.

The 31-year-old helps creates the sweet treats and even makes coffees at his venues.

He has just over 16k followers on Instagram, through which he advertises his business, and shows off buff gym selfies.

An Min-young

An Min-Young joins the rest of the class as a Pilates instructor.

The 26-year-old successfully runs her own studio and also models on the side.

She was crowned Miss Korea Busan in 2023.

Lee Gwan-hee

Lee Gwan-hee is the oldest contestant in Single's Inferno this year, at 36.

He is a pro-basketball player for the LG Sakers and boasts 45.2k followers on social media.

The shooting guard has also garnered an impressive following on YouTube where he documents his sporting career.

Son Won-ik

Son Won-ik works as a real estate agent and his social media suggests he also enjoys boxing as a hobby.

The 29-year-old described himself as optimistic with a "boyish charm".

His Instagram account boats 21.2k followers and his posts reveal he has also completed stints as a model.

Yun Ha-bin

Yun Ha-bin is an actor who currently features in the SBS series My Demon.

He plays a minor role in the show and not much else is known about him.

The 29-year-old claimed he wanted to "shake things up" on Single's Inferno this season.

Yun Ha-jeong

Yun Ha-jeong works for a medical clothing company and as a model.

The 26-year-old is in charge of customer service at the firm.

She has attracted 45k fans on Instagram, where she posted travel and personal blogs.

Park Min-kyu

Park Min-kyu has showed off his strong physique on the show, useful for his career in the police force.

He is part of the Korea Coast Guard Special Rescue Team and has demonstrated his strong swimming abilities

The 39-year-old made a bold entrance and made it clear he heavily peruses the girls he's interested in.

Yu Si-eun

Yu Si-eun has remained very mysterious, not revealing her age or profession.

However, she was a runner up on Miss Korea 2022 and her social media suggests she does modelling.

Her posts have amassed 101k followers and she has recently launched a YouTube account.
