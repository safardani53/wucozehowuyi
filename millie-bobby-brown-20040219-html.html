Millie Bobby Brown is a British actress and producer. She gained recognition for playing Eleven in the Netflix science fiction series Stranger Things, which earned her two Primetime Emmy Award nominations for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series, and two Screen Actors Guild Award nominations for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Drama Series. She is one of the youngest nominees for the award. In 2018, Brown was featured in the Time 100 list of the world's most influential people, and was appointed as a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador, the then youngest person selected for this position. Brown made her feature film debut with a lead role in Godzilla: King of the Monsters, a character she reprised in the sequel Godzilla vs. Kong. She also starred in and produced the Netflix mystery film Enola Holmes.
