Know about Lil Boosies Four Daughters: Iviona, Tarlaysia, Lyric and Toriana

Publish date: 2024-02-06

In his personal life, American rapper Lil Boosie, who was arrested in San Diego on gun charges in May 2023, has been dating Rajel Nelson.

Nelson is an entrepreneur, actress, and fashionista. She is over 12 years younger than him and met him when she was 18 years old outside her job location (IHOP) in a tourist area.

Boosie's girlfriend Nelson has worked in the movies such as On Angel's Wings and Kiss The Bride. She currently runs her own beauty and lifestyle brand of her name. 

Boosie was born on 14 November 14, 1982 (age 40 years), in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, United States, whereas his girlfriend Nelson was born on 8 April 1995.

The pair share an intimate relationship and have an immense respect for one another. 

According to sources, his girlfriend keeps him grounded but totally respects his point of view and caring nature. 

In 2022, it was revealed by Badazz he made Nelson remove her breast implants but Nelson insisted to The Progressive Report that she had to remove it for her own reason (after she developed a cyst there).

“I bought some ti**ies,” Boosie said. “Then one of my b***hes, my new b***h, we had broke up, she went and got her titties done and when we got back together, I made her go back and take them out,” he told DJ Vlad. “I think it was to get back at me. She knew I didn’t want that. She had got like some… I don’t know. They wasn’t much bigger because I didn’t notice ’em. I didn’t notice ’em at first. She had fell and I noticed them… But she did it out of spite."

Previously, the Baton Rogue native has had numerous relationships.

If you didn't know, he has eight children with six women. Out of his eight children, four of them are daughters.

He has shown a lot of love for his kids over the years. When he was released from prison back in 2014, he said he was enjoying spending more time with his kids.

“I’m still enjoying the moment, enjoying my family, you know? I’m just soaking in time with the kids and the studio,” he said.

Here's a look at Boosie’s four daughters and what’s known about them.

Iviona Hatch

Like Boosie, his eldest daughter, Iviona Hatch, is also a rapper. She was born out of his relationship with Walnita Decuir.

She started rapping and writing songs when she was just 9 and goes by the name Poison Ivi.

She was born on 4 December 2001 and is 21 years old as of now. 

During an interview with DJ Smallz Eyes 2, she said she had been passionate about rapping for a long time but did not commit to it because she thought she would primarily play basketball.

However, she later changed her mind because athletes in WNBA did not make nearly as much as the athletes in NBA.

Iviona also shared that Boosie didn’t push her toward becoming a rapper and that it was her own choice.

“He [Boosie] taught me how to play basketball… So I always thought he wanted me to keep pushing with basketball,” she said.

“He knew I knew how to do [rap], but he thought I would take basketball more seriously.”

Iviona frequently uploads her songs and music videos to her YouTube channel, where she has amassed over 52k plus subscribers.

In 2023, she revealed her girlfriend on Instagram, writing “Endlessly in love with you."

She had previously come out as gay during an Instagram Live in 2021, revealing that she sometimes dated women, according to MadameNoire. 

However, her father Boosie is not content with her being lesbian but still "loves" her.

"But what she does, we’re gonna love her to death. But at the same time, our Southern family has never been that way," he shared with Vlad TV, according to HipHopDx. “It’s never been accepted. But at the same time, she’s grown and she can do what the hell she wants. She’s old enough to know what she wants and that’s her.”

 Boosie added that his daughter didn't tell her about her sexuality in person.

“I learned of it through social media. Like you, I woke up to it… She never came to me and talked to me about relationships," he said.

The court later dismissed her case because she failed to show up in court.
